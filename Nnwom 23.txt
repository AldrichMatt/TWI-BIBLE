Dwom Dawid dwom.Awurade ne me hwɛfo, hwee renhia me.

Ɔma meda wura frɔmfrɔm adidibea;ogya me kɔ nsu a ɛho dwo ho,
/// Nnwom 23:3
odwudwo me kra.Ɔde me fa akwantrenee so,ne din nti.

Sɛ menamowu sum kabii mu mpo a,minsuro bɔne bi,efisɛ wo ne me na ɛwɔ hɔ;wʼabaa ne wo pemana ɛkyekye me werɛ.
/// Nnwom 23:5
Woto me pon wɔmʼatamfo anim.Wode ngo fɔw me tirim;me kuruwa yɛ ma bu so.
/// Nnwom 23:6
Yiye ne adɔe nko na ebedi mʼakyime nkwanna nyinaa;na mɛtena Awurade fidaa.

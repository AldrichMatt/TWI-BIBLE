/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:1
Nnamfonom:Ɛhe na wo dɔfo no kɔmmea ahoɔfɛfo mu ahoɔfɛ?Ɛhe na wo dɔfo man fae,na yɛne wo nkɔhwehwɛ no?
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:2
Ababaa:Me dɔfo kɔ ne turo mu,faako a woduadua nnua ahorow,okokyinkyin turo no muakɔboaboa sukooko ano.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:3
Me dɔfo yɛ me de, na me nso meyɛ ne de;okyinkyin sukooko no mu.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:4
Aberante:Me dɔfo, wo ho yɛ fɛ te sɛ Tirsa,wʼahoɔfɛ te sɛ Yerusalem,wusi pi te sɛ asraafo a wɔretu frankaa.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:5
Nhwɛ me saa;wobunkam me so.Wo ti nwi te sɛ mpapokuw awɔresian afi Gilead.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:6
Wo se te sɛ nguankuw a wɔatwitwa wɔn ho nwi foforo,a wofi aguaree.Baako mpo nyeraa ɛ.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:7
Wʼasontɔre a ɛhyɛ wo nkataanim mute sɛ ntunkum aduaba fa.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:8
Ebia na ɔyerenom yɛ aduosia,mpenanom bɛyɛ aduɔwɔtwe,ne mmabaa dodow a wontumi nkan wɔn;
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:9
nanso mʼaborɔnoma a ne ho nni asɛm yɛ sononko;ɔno nko ara ne ne na babea,ɔno na nea ɔwoo no no pɛ nʼasɛm.Mmabaa huu no no, wɔfrɛɛ no nhyira;ahemmea ne mpenanom kamfoo no.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:10
Nnamfonom:Hena na wapue sɛ ahemadakye yi,ɔyɛ frɔmfrɔm sɛ ɔsram, na ɔhyerɛn sɛ owia,nʼanuonyam te sɛ nsoromma a wɔsa so.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:11
Aberante:Misian kɔɔ nnuaba pɔw mu hɔsɛ merekɔhwehwɛ afifide foforo a ɛwɔ obon no mu,sɛ bobe no agu nhwiren,anaasɛ ntunkum no ayɛ frɔmfrɔm.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:12
Mʼani baa me ho so nona mʼadwene de me abesi me nkurɔfo adehye nteaseɛnam so.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 6:13
Nnamfonom:San wʼakyi, san wʼakyi, Sulamit babea;San bra, san bra ma yɛnhwɛ wo!Aberante:Adɛn nti na ɛsɛ sɛ mohwɛ Sulamit abeawasɛnea mohwɛ Mahanaim asaw no?

/// Hesekiel 19:1
Israel Mmapɔmma Ho Kwadwom“Momma Israel mmapɔmma ho kwadwom 
/// Hesekiel 19:2
na monka se:“ ‘Na wo na yɛ gyatabere mu sononkowɔ gyata mu!Ɔdaa gyataforo muyɛn ne mma.
/// Hesekiel 19:3
Ɔyɛn ne mma no mu baakoma ɔbɛyɛɛ gyata ɔhoɔdenfo.Osuaa sɛnea wɔtetew hanam mu,na ɔwee nnipa nam.
/// Hesekiel 19:4
Amanaman no tee ne nka,na wɔn amoa yii no.Wɔkyekyeree no de no kɔɔMisraim asase so.
/// Hesekiel 19:5
“ ‘Bere a ohuu sɛ nʼanidaso anyɛ hɔ,na nea ɔrehwɛ anim ayera no,ɔfaa ne ba foforoyɛɛ no gyata ɔhoɔdenfo.
/// Hesekiel 19:6
Okyinkyin gyata no muefisɛ na wayɛ gyata hoɔdenfo.Osuaa sɛnea wɔtetew hanam muna ɔwee nnipa nam.
/// Hesekiel 19:7
Obubuu wɔn abandennenna ɔsɛee wɔn nkurow.Asase no ne wɔn a wɔte so nyinaa no,ne mmubomu bɔɔ wɔn hu.
/// Hesekiel 19:8
Afei amanaman no sɔre tiaa no,wɔn a wofi nsase a atwa ahyia no so.Woguu no asau kyeree nowɔ wɔn amoa mu.
/// Hesekiel 19:9
Wɔde nnarewa twee no kɔhyɛɛ buw muna wɔde no brɛɛ Babilonia hene.Wɔde no too afiase,na wɔante ne mmubomu no biowɔ Israel mmepɔw no so.
/// Hesekiel 19:10
“ ‘Na wo na te sɛ bobeduaa wɔatɛw no asu ho wɔ wo bobeturo mu.Na ɛsow aba, na eyiyii mmannsu a onya no bebree no nti.
/// Hesekiel 19:11
Ne mman yɛɛ den yiye;edi mu sɛ wɔde yɛ ahemfo ahempema.Ɛkorɔn, bunkam faa nhaban a ayɛ kuhaa no so,ne sorokɔ ne ne mman bebree nomaa no yɛɛ sononko.
/// Hesekiel 19:12
Nanso wɔde abufuwhyew tuu nʼasede no hwee fam.Apuei mframa maa no kasae,ɛsow aba tetew gui;na ne mman a ɛwɔ ahoɔden no twintwameena ogya hyewee.
/// Hesekiel 19:13
Afei wɔakɔtɛw no wɔ sare soasase kesee a nsu nni mu mu.
/// Hesekiel 19:14
Ogya trɛw fii ne mman baako sona ɛhyew nʼaba.Anka mman a ɛwɔ ahoɔden baako mpo wɔ so;nea wɔde yɛ ahemfo ahempema ma ɛyɛ yiye no.’Eyi yɛ kwadwom na ma wɔnto no saa.”

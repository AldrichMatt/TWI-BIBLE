/// Nnwom 26:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Bu me bem, Awurade,efisɛ, mabɔ bra a asɛm nni ho:mede me ho too Awurade sona manhinhim.
/// Nnwom 26:2
Sɔ me hwɛ, Awurade,hwehwɛ me koma ne mʼadwene mu.
/// Nnwom 26:3
Wʼadɔe wɔ mʼanim daana mɛnantew wo nokware mu.
/// Nnwom 26:4
Me ne nnaadaafo ntenana me ne nyaatwomfo nso nni hwee yɛ.
/// Nnwom 26:5
Mikyi nnebɔneyɛfo guabɔna mempɛ sɛ me ne amumɔyɛfo tena.
/// Nnwom 26:6
Mehohoro me nsa wɔ bembu mu,na mekɔ wʼafɔremuka ho ahyia, Awurade,
/// Nnwom 26:7
na mepae mu ka wʼayeyina meka wʼanwonwade nyinaa.
/// Nnwom 26:8
Mʼani gye ofi a wote mu ho, Awurade,faako a wʼanuonyam wɔ no.
/// Nnwom 26:9
Mfa me kra nka nnebɔneyɛfo de ho nkɔ,ne me nkwa nka mogyapɛfo de ho,
/// Nnwom 26:10
wɔn a amumɔyɛ nhyehyɛe wɔ wɔn nsamna adanmude ayɛ wɔn nsa nifa ma.
/// Nnwom 26:11
Nanso mebɔ ɔbra a ho tew;hu me mmɔbɔ na gye me nkwa.
/// Nnwom 26:12
Mʼanan sisi asase tamaa so;na guabɔ kɛse ase na mɛkamfo Awurade.

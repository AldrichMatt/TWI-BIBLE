/// Yesaia 50:1
Israel Amumɔyɛ Ne Ɔsomfo No SetieSɛɛ na Awurade se:“Ɛhe na awaregyae ho adansedi nhoma,nea migyinaa so gyaa no kwan no wɔ?Anaa sɛ mʼadefirifo mu henana metɔn wo maa no?Wo bɔne nti na wɔtɔn wo;Wʼamumɔyɛ nti na wɔpam wo na.
/// Yesaia 50:2
Mebae no, adɛn nti na na obiara nni hɔ?Mefrɛe no, adɛn nti na obiara annye so?Na me nsa yɛ tia dodo sɛ ebegye wo ana?Minni ahoɔden a mede begye wo ana?Mede mʼanimka kɛkɛ no ma po yow,medan nsubɔnten nweatammu mpataa porɔw esiane nsu a wonnya ntina osukɔm kum wɔn.
/// Yesaia 50:3
Mede sum fura ɔsorona mema atweaatam yɛ ne nkataho.”
/// Yesaia 50:4
Asafo Awurade ama me nkyerɛkyerɛ tɛkrɛmasɛ ɛnka asɛm a ɛhyɛ ɔbrɛfo den.Onyan me anɔpa biara,na obue mʼaso ma mitie sɛ obi a ɔregye nkyerɛkyerɛ.
/// Yesaia 50:5
Otumfo Awurade abue mʼasona menyɛɛ otuatewfo ɛ;na mensan mʼakyi ɛ.
/// Yesaia 50:6
Mede mʼakyi maa wɔn a wɔboro me,ne mʼafono maa wɔn a wotutu mʼabogyesɛ.Mamfa mʼanim anhintaw wɔnfɛwdi ne ntasu ho.
/// Yesaia 50:7
Esiane sɛ, Asafo Awurade boa me nti,wɔrengu mʼanim ase.Enti na mayɛ mʼanim sɛ ɔtwɛrebo,na minim sɛ mʼanim rengu ase.
/// Yesaia 50:8
Nea obu me bem no abɛn.Enti hena na ɔbɛbɔ me kwaadu?Ɔmma yenhyia mu!Hena na ɔbɔ me sobo?Ma ɔnka nsi mʼanim!
/// Yesaia 50:9
Asafo Awurade na ɔboa me.Hena ne nea obebu me fɔ?Wɔn nyinaa bɛtetew sɛ atade;nnwewemmoa bɛwe wɔn.
/// Yesaia 50:10
Mo mu hena na osuro Awurade,na odi ne somfo asɛm so?Ma nea ɔnam sum munea onni hann no,onnye Awurade din nnina ɔmfa ne ho nto ne Nyankopɔn so.
/// Yesaia 50:11
Na afei, mo a mososɔ ogya nyinaana moyɛ ogyatɛn ma mo ho,monkɔ, nkɔnantew mo gya hann no,ne ogyatɛn a wɔasɔ ano no mu.Eyi ne nea mubenya afi me nsa mu;mobɛdeda fam wɔ ɔyaw mu.

/// Nnwom 127:1
Dwom Ɔsoroforo dwom. Salomo de.Sɛ Awurade ansi ɔdan no a,adansifo no yɛ ho adwuma kwa.Sɛ Awurade ammɔ kuropɔn no ho ban a,awɛmfo no wɛn kwa.
/// Nnwom 127:2
Sɛ wosɔre anɔpahemana wotena kosi anadwo,na woyɛ adwumaden pɛ wʼano aduan a,ɛyɛ ɔkwa, efisɛ wɔn a ɔdɔ wɔn no, ɔma wɔn wɔ wɔn nnae mu.
/// Nnwom 127:3
Mma yɛ agyapade a efi Awurade hɔ,na mma yɛ akatua a efi no.
/// Nnwom 127:4
Mma a wɔwo wɔn mmabun bere mu note sɛ agyan a ɛhyɛ ɔsraani nsam.
/// Nnwom 127:5
Nhyira ne onipa anʼagyan kotoku ayɛ ma.Nʼanim rengu asebere a wahyia nʼatamfo wɔ kurow pon ano no.

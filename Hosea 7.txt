/// Hosea 7:1
bere biara a mɛsa Israel yare no,Efraim nnebɔne da adi.Samaria awurukasɛm nso da adi.Wɔyɛ nnaadaafoakorɔmfo a wobubu adan ani kɔ afi mu,ne akwamukafo a wɔbɔ korɔn wɔ mmɔnten so.
/// Hosea 7:2
Na wonhu sɛmekae wɔn nnebɔne nyinaa.Wɔn bɔne bunkam wɔn so;egu mʼanim bere biara.
/// Hosea 7:3
“Wɔde atirimɔdensɛm gyigye ɔhene no ani,na wɔde atoro nso gyigye ahenemma no ani.
/// Hosea 7:4
Wɔn nyinaa yɛ aguamammɔfoa akɔnnɔ redɛw wɔ wɔn mu.Wɔte sɛ fononoo a wɔasɔ mu gyabere a brodotofo ahyɛ ase refɔtɔw nʼasikresiam.
/// Hosea 7:5
Da a ɔhene rehyɛ fa noahenemma bow nsa,na ɔhene no fɛwdifo ne wɔn bɔ mu goru.
/// Hosea 7:6
Wɔn koma te sɛ fononoo;wɔde atirimpɔw bɔne kɔ ɔhene anim.Wɔn nsusuwii bɔne no dɛɛdɛɛ anadwo mu nyinaa;anɔpa, ɛdɛw te sɛ ogyatannaa.
/// Hosea 7:7
Wɔn nyinaa ayɛ hyew sɛ fononoo.Wokunkum wɔn sodifo.Wɔn ahemfo nyinaa hwehwe asena wɔn mu biara nsu mfrɛ me.
/// Hosea 7:8
“Efraim ne aman no adi afra;Efraim yɛ ɔfam a anyinya a wonkisae.
/// Hosea 7:9
Ananafo twe nʼahoɔden,nanso onhu.Dwen aba ne ti so,nanso nʼani mmaa so ɛ.
/// Hosea 7:10
Israel ahantan di adanse tia no,nanso eyi nyinaa akyi noɔnnsan nkɔ Awurade ne Nyankopɔn nkyɛn,na ɔnhwehwɛ no nso.
/// Hosea 7:11
“Efraim te sɛ aborɔnoma,wotumi daadaa no na onni adwene.Ɔfrɛ Misraim,na wadan akɔ Asiria.
/// Hosea 7:12
Sɛ wɔkɔ a, mɛtow mʼasau agu wɔn so;atwe wɔn aba fam te sɛ wim nnomaa.Sɛ mete sɛ wɔretu abɔ mu a,mɛkyere wɔn.
/// Hosea 7:13
Nnome nka wɔn,efisɛ wɔatwe wɔn ho afi me ho!Ɔsɛe mmra wɔn soefisɛ wɔatew me so atua!anka mepɛ sɛ migye wɔnnanso wutwa atoro fa me ho.
/// Hosea 7:14
Wommfi wɔn koma mu nsu mfrɛ me,mmom wotwa adwo wɔ wɔn mpa so.Wɔboaboa wɔn ho ano kɔhwehwɛ aduan ne nsa foforo,nanso wɔtwe wɔn ho fi me ho.
/// Hosea 7:15
Metetew wɔn, hyɛɛ wɔn den,nanso wɔbɔ pɔw bɔne tia me.
/// Hosea 7:16
Wɔnhwehwɛ Ɔsorosoroni no;wɔte sɛ tadua a asɛe,wɔn ntuanofo bɛtotɔ wɔ afoa anoefisɛ, wɔnkasa obu so.Eyi nti wobedi wɔn ho fɛwwɔ Misraim asase so.

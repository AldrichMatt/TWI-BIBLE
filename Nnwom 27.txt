/// Nnwom 27:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Awurade ne me hann ne me nkwagye;hena na minsuro no?Awurade ne me nkwa ahoɔden,na hena anim na me ho mpopo?
/// Nnwom 27:2
Sɛ nnipa bɔne bɔ toa mepɛ sɛ wokum me,sɛ mʼatamfo tow hyɛ me so a,wobehintihintiw ahwehwe ase.
/// Nnwom 27:3
Sɛ dɔm mpo twa me ho hyia a,me koma rentu;mpo sɛ wotu me so sa a,ɛno mu koraa na minya awerehyɛmu.
/// Nnwom 27:4
Ade baako na mibisa Awurade;na ɛno na mehwehwɛ;sɛ mɛtena Awurade fime nkwanna nyinaa,na mahwɛ Awurade ahoɔfɛna mahwehwɛ no wɔ nʼasɔrefi.
/// Nnwom 27:5
Hiada mu no,ɔde me besie nʼatenae dwoodwoo;ɔbɛkora me wɔ Ahyiae Ntamadan kronkron muɔde me si ɔbotan so wɔ sorosoro.
/// Nnwom 27:6
Afei ɔbɛma me ti sowɔ atamfo a wɔatwa me ho ahyia no so;mede anigye bɛbɔ afɔre wɔ nʼAhyiae Ntamadan mu;na mɛto dwom, ahyehyɛ nnwom ama Awurade.
/// Nnwom 27:7
Awurade, sɛ misu frɛ wo a, tie me!Hu me mmɔbɔ na gye me so!
/// Nnwom 27:8
Me koma ka wɔ wo ho se, “Hwehwɛ nʼanim!”Wʼanim, Awurade na mɛhwehwɛ.
/// Nnwom 27:9
Mfa wʼanim nhintaw me,mfa abufuw mpam wo somfome boafo ne wo.Mpo me, na nnyaw me,mʼAgyenkwa Nyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 27:10
Mʼagya ne me na begyaw me hɔ de,nanso Awurade begye me.
/// Nnwom 27:11
Kyerɛ me wʼakwan, Awurade;fa me fa ɔkwan tee so,me nhyɛsofo nti.
/// Nnwom 27:12
Nnyaa me mma sɛnea mʼatamfo pɛ,nnansekurumfo sɔre tia mena wopuw awurukasɛm.
/// Nnwom 27:13
Meda so wɔ saa anidaso yi sɛ:mehu Awurade ayamyewɔ ateasefo asase so.
/// Nnwom 27:14
Twɛn Awurade.Hyɛ wo ho den na ma wo bo nyɛ duruna twɛn Awurade.

Onyankopɔn Ne AhoniMuntie nea Awurade ka kyerɛ mo, Israelfi. 
/// Yeremia 10:2
Sɛɛ na Awurade se:“Munnsua amanaman no akwananaa momma wim nsɛnkyerɛnne mmɔ mo hu,ɛwɔ mu sɛ wɔbɔ amanaman no hu.

/// Yeremia 10:4

Ɛte sɛ kaakaabotoni a esi ɛfere mfikyifuw mu,wɔn ahoni no ntumi nkasa;ɛsɛ sɛ wɔsoa wɔnefisɛ wontumi nnantew.Munnsuro wɔn;wɔrentumi nhaw mona wɔrentumi nyɛ ade pa biara nso.”
/// Yeremia 10:6
Obiara nte sɛ wo, Awurade;woyɛ ɔkɛsena wo din so wɔ tumi mu.
/// Yeremia 10:7

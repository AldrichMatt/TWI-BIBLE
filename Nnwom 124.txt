/// Nnwom 124:1
Dwom Ɔsoroforo dwom. Dawid de.Sɛ anka Awurade nni yɛn afa a,ma Israel nka,
/// Nnwom 124:2
Sɛ anka Awurade nni yɛn afabere a nnipa tow hyɛɛ yɛn so,
/// Nnwom 124:3
bere a wɔn abufuw sɔre tiaa yɛn aanka wɔmemenee yɛn anikann;
/// Nnwom 124:4
anka nsuyiri no bebu afa yɛn so,anka ɔhweam no bɛtwe yɛn akɔ,
/// Nnwom 124:5
anka nsu a ano yɛ den nobɛpra yɛn kɔ.
/// Nnwom 124:6
Ayeyi nka Awurade awamma ho kwan amma wɔamfa wɔn se antetew yɛn mu.
/// Nnwom 124:7
Yɛaguan sɛ anomaaafi ofirisumfo afiri mu;wɔabubu afiri no,ama yɛaguan.
/// Nnwom 124:8
Yɛn mmoa wɔ Awurade din mu,ɔsoro ne asase Yɛfo no.

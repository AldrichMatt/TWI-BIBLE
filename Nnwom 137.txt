/// Nnwom 137:1
Dwom Babilonia nsubɔnten ho, ɛhɔ na yɛtena suubere a yɛkaee Sion no.
/// Nnwom 137:2
Ɛhɔ konkɔn so nnua muna yɛde yɛn asanku sensɛnee,
/// Nnwom 137:3
ɛhɔ na yɛn nnommumfafo kaa se yɛnto nnwom,yɛn ahohiafo hwehwɛɛ anigye nnwom fii yɛn nkyɛn;wɔkae se, “Monto Sion nnwom baako nkyerɛ yɛn!”
/// Nnwom 137:4
Ɛbɛyɛ dɛn na yɛatumi ato Awurade nnwom,bere a yɛwɔ ananafo asase so?
/// Nnwom 137:5
Yerusalem, sɛ me werɛ fi wo ama me nsa nifa werɛ mfi sankubɔ.
/// Nnwom 137:6
Sɛ mankae wo a,ma me tɛkrɛma nka ntare mʼanomsɛ mamfa Yerusalemsɛ mʼanigyede kɛse pa ara a.
/// Nnwom 137:7
Kae, Awurade, nea Edomfo yɛɛda a Yerusalem hwee ase no.Wɔteɛɛ mu se, “Munnwiriw,munnwiriw ngu fam nkɔka ne fapem!”
/// Nnwom 137:8
Ɔbabea Babilonia, wɔadome wo akɔ ɔsɛe mu,nea otua wo ka bio ani gyewɔ nea wode ayɛ yɛn no ho.
/// Nnwom 137:9
Nea ɔkyere wo nkokoaade wɔn dwiradwira abotan no ani gye.

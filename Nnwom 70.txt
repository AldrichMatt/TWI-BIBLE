/// Nnwom 70:1
Dwom Dawid adesrɛ dwom.Ao, Onyankopɔn, yɛ ntɛm gye me nkwa;Awurade bra ntɛm bɛboa me.
/// Nnwom 70:2
Ma wɔn a wɔhwehwɛ me kra noanim ngu ase na wɔnyɛ basaa;ma wɔn a wɔhwehwɛ me sɛe no nyinaamfa animguase nsan wɔn akyi.
/// Nnwom 70:3
Ma wɔn a wɔka, “Ɔtwea! Ɔtwea!” kyerɛ me nonsan wɔn akyi, esiane wɔn aniwu nti.
/// Nnwom 70:4
Nanso ma wɔn a wɔhwehwɛ wo nyinaansɛpɛw wɔn ho na wɔn ani nnye wɔ wo mu;ma wɔn a wɔdɔ wo nkwagye no nka da biara se,“Onyankopɔn yɛ ɔkɛse!”
/// Nnwom 70:5
Me de meyɛ ohiani a minni ɔboafo;Ao, Onyankopɔn, bra me nkyɛn ntɛm.Wo ne me boafo ne me gyefo;Awurade, ntwentwɛn wʼanan ase.

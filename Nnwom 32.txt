/// Nnwom 32:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Nhyira ne wɔn awɔde wɔn amumɔyɛ akyɛ wɔn,na wɔakata wɔn bɔne so.
/// Nnwom 32:2
Nhyira nka onipa aAwurade mmu ne bɔne ngu no sona nnaadaa nni ne honhom mu.
/// Nnwom 32:3
Bere a meyɛɛ komm no,me nnompe yɛɛ mmrɛw,apini a misii no da mu nyinaa nti.
/// Nnwom 32:4
Awia ne anadwowo nsa yɛɛ den wɔ me so;mʼahoɔden saete sɛ awia bere mu ɔhyew.
/// Nnwom 32:5
Afei, migyee me bɔne too mu.Mankata mʼamumɔyɛ so.Mekae se, “Mɛkame bɔne akyerɛ Awurade,”na wode me bɔne ho afɔdi kyɛɛ me.
/// Nnwom 32:6
Enti, ma agyidifo nyinaa mmɔ wo mpaebere a wohu wo;ampa ara sɛ asu akɛse yiri a,ɛrenka no.
/// Nnwom 32:7
Wone me hintabea;wobɛbɔ me ho ban wɔ amanehunu muna wode nkwagye nnwom atwa me ho ahyia.
/// Nnwom 32:8
Mɛkyerɛkyerɛ wo ɔkwan a wobɛfa sometu wo fo na mahwɛ wo so.
/// Nnwom 32:9
Ɛnyɛ sɛ ɔpɔnkɔ anaa afurumpɔnkɔ aonni ntease ntiwɔde nnareka ne hama na ɛdannan no,sɛ ɛbɛyɛ a wɔbɛba wo nkyɛn.
/// Nnwom 32:10
Omumɔyɛfo amanehunu dɔɔso,nanso Awurade adɔe a ɛnsa da notwa onipa a ɔde ne ho to no so no ho hyia.
/// Nnwom 32:11
Munni ahurusi na mo ani nnye wɔ Awurade mu, mo atreneefo;monto dwom, mo a mo koma mu tew.

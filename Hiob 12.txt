/// Hiob 12:1
Hiob Bua SofarNa Hiob buae se,
/// Hiob 12:2
“Akyinnye nni ho, mo ne nnipa nona muwu a na nyansa asa!
/// Hiob 12:3
Nanso mewɔ adwene sɛ mo aramonnsen me.Hena na onnim saa nneɛma yi nyinaa?
/// Hiob 12:4
“Mayɛ aserewde ama me nnamfonom,mefrɛɛ Onyankopɔn na obuae,aserewde teta bi, nanso meteɛ na me ho nni asɛm.
/// Hiob 12:5
Nnipa a wɔn ho tɔ wɔn no bu amanehunu animtiaasɛnea wobu wɔn a wɔn nan rewatiri no awiei animtiaa no.
/// Hiob 12:6
Wɔmmfa wɔn nsa nka akorɔmfo ntamadan,na wɔn a woyi Onyankopɔn abufuw wɔ bammɔna Onyankopɔn bɔ nea ɔde ma wɔn ho ban.
/// Hiob 12:7
“Bisa mmoa no, na wɔbɛkyerɛ woanaasɛ wim nnomaa no, na wɔbɛka akyerɛ wo
/// Hiob 12:8
anaasɛ kasa kyerɛ asase, na ɛbɛkyerɛ wo,anaasɛ ma po mu mpataa nka nkyerɛ wo.
/// Hiob 12:9
Wɔn nyinaa mu hena na onnim sɛAwurade nsa na ayɛ eyi.
/// Hiob 12:10
Ne nsam na abɔde nyinaa home wɔne adesamma nyinaa home.
/// Hiob 12:11
So aso nsɔ nsɛm nhwɛsɛnea tɛkrɛma ka aduan hwɛ no ana?
/// Hiob 12:12
Wonnya nyansa mfi mpanyin nkyɛn,na ɛnyɛ onyinkyɛ na ɛde ntease ba ana?
/// Hiob 12:13
“Nyansa ne tumi yɛ Onyankopɔn de;afotu ne ntease yɛ ne dea.
/// Hiob 12:14
Nea obubu gu no, obiara rentumi nsi;nea ɔde no to afiase no, obiara rentumi nyi no.
/// Hiob 12:15
Sɛ ɔma osu gyae tɔ a, asase no so yɛ wosee;sɛ ogyaa osutɔ mu a, ɛsɛe asase no.
/// Hiob 12:16
Ahoɔden ne nkonimdi yɛ ne deosisifo ne nea wosisi no no nso yɛ ne de.
/// Hiob 12:17
Ɔpa afotufo ho ntama ma wɔkɔna ɔma atemmufo yɛ nkwasea.
/// Hiob 12:18
Ɔworɔw ahemfo nkyehamana ɔde abɔso bɔ wɔn asen.
/// Hiob 12:19
Ɔpa asɔfo ho ntama,na otu nnipa a wɔn ase atim akyɛ gu.
/// Hiob 12:20
Ɔka afotufo a wogye wɔn di no kasaboa hyɛ,na ogye mpanyimfo nhumu.
/// Hiob 12:21
Ɔde animguase gu atitiriw sona otintim gye ahoɔdenfo nsam akode.
/// Hiob 12:22
Ɔda sum mu nneɛma a ahintaw adina ɔde sum kabii ba hann mu.
/// Hiob 12:23
Ɔyɛ aman akɛse, na ɔsɛe wɔn;ɔtrɛw aman mu, na ɔhwete wɔn mu.
/// Hiob 12:24
Ogye wiase akannifo adwene fi wɔn nsam;na ɔma wokyinkyin asase wosee a ɔkwan nna so so.
/// Hiob 12:25
Wɔkeka wɔ sum mu a wonni kanea;na ɔma wɔtɔ ntintan sɛ asabowfo.”

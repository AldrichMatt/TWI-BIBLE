/// Yesaia 14:1
Awurade behu Yakob mmɔbɔ;ɔbɛsan afa Israelna ɔbɛbɔ wɔn atenase wɔ wɔn ankasa asase so.Ananafo bɛba abɛka wɔn hona wɔne Yakobfi aka abɔ mu.
/// Yesaia 14:2
Amanaman bɛfa wɔnde wɔn aba wɔn ankasa atenae.Na Israelfi bɛfa amanaman no ayɛ wɔn desɛ nkoa ne mfenaa wɔ Awurade asase so.Wɔbɛyɛ wɔn a wɔyɛɛ wɔn nnommumfo no nnommumna wobedi wɔn sohwɛfo so.
/// Yesaia 14:3
Na da a Awurade bɛma mo ɔhome afi amanehunu, ahoyeraw ne nkoasom dennen mu no, 
/// Yesaia 14:4
mubedi Babiloniahene ho fɛw se:hwɛ sɛnea sohyɛfo no aba nʼawie!ne sɛnea nʼabufuw nso to atwa!
/// Yesaia 14:5
Awurade abubu otirimɔdenfo no abaa mu,sodifo ahempema no,
/// Yesaia 14:6
nea abufuw mu no ɔde bobɔɔ wɔntoatoaa so hwehwee fam,na abufuwhyew mu otintim wɔn so a ahomegye nni mude brɛɛ amanaman no ase no.
/// Yesaia 14:7
Nsase no nyinaa rehome wɔ asomdwoe mu;na wɔto dwom.
/// Yesaia 14:8
Mpo, ɔpepaw nnua ne Lebanon sidanya ho anigye na wɔka se,“Afei a wɔabrɛ wo ase yi,duatwafo biara remmetwa yɛn.”
/// Yesaia 14:9
Awufo nna a ɛwɔ ase no rekeka ne hoabehyia wo kwan;ɛkanyan wɔn a wɔafi mu no honhomsɛ wɔmmɛma wo akwaaba,wɔn a na wɔyɛ akannifo wɔ wiaseɛma wɔsɔre fi wɔn nhengua so,wɔn a na wɔyɛ ahemfo wɔ amanaman so.
/// Yesaia 14:10
Wɔn nyinaa begye so,wɔbɛka akyerɛ wo se,“Wo nso woayɛ mmerɛw sɛ yɛn;woayɛ sɛ yɛn ara.”
/// Yesaia 14:11
Wo kɛseyɛ nyinaa wɔde aba ɔda mu,ne huuyɛ a efi wo asanku no;wɔde nsaammoa asɛw kɛtɛ ama wona asunson akata wo so.
/// Yesaia 14:12
Sɛnea wufi ɔsoro abɛhwe fam,anɔpa nsoromma, adekyee babarima!Wɔato wo abɛhwe asase so,wo a na kan no wobrɛ amanaman ase!
/// Yesaia 14:13
Wokaa no wo koma mu se,“Mɛforo akɔ ɔsoro;mɛma mʼahengua soasi Onyankopɔn nsoromma atifi;mɛtena ase adi hene wɔ ahyiae bepɔw so,nea ɛkorɔn pa ara wɔ bepɔw kronkron no so.
/// Yesaia 14:14
Mɛforo atra omununkum atifi;mɛyɛ me ho sɛ Ɔsorosoroni no.”
/// Yesaia 14:15
Nanso wɔde wo abɛto ɔda mu,amoa donkudonku ase tɔnn.
/// Yesaia 14:16
Wɔn a wuhu wo no hwɛ wo haw,wodwen nea ato wo no ho bisa se,“Ɛnyɛ ɔbarima a ɔwosow asasena ɔmaa ahenni ho popoe,
/// Yesaia 14:17
ɔbarima a ɔmaa wiase dan nweatam,nea ɔdan ne nkuropɔn butuwiina wamma ne nneduafo ankɔ wɔn nkyi no ni?”
/// Yesaia 14:18
Amanaman ahemfo no nyinaa wɔadeda wɔnobiara wɔ ne da mu.
/// Yesaia 14:19
Nanso wɔapam wo afi wo da mute sɛ dubaa a wɔapo;wɔde wɔn a wɔatotɔ akata wo so,wɔn a wɔtɔɔ wɔ afoa ano,wɔn a wosian kɔ abo amoa mu,te sɛ funu a wɔatiatia so,
/// Yesaia 14:20
Wɔrensie wo nka wɔn ho,efisɛ woasɛe wʼasasena wakunkum wo nkurɔfo.Otirimɔdenfo asefo nowɔrenkae wɔn bio. Wo babarima renni wʼade sɛ ɔhene.
/// Yesaia 14:21
Siesie faako a wobekunkum ne mmabarimawɔ wɔn agyanom bɔne nti;ɛnsɛ sɛ wɔsɔre di asase no sona wɔde wɔn nkuropɔn pete so.
/// Yesaia 14:22
“Mɛsɔre atia wɔn,”Sɛnea Asafo Awurade se ni.“Metwa ne din ne ne nkae,ne mma ne ne nenanom afi Babilonia ho,”sɛnea Asafo Awurade se ni.
/// Yesaia 14:23
“Mɛdan no ayɛ faako a mpatu wɔne ɔwora;mede ɔsɛeprae bɛpra no,”sɛnea Asafo Awurade se ni.
/// Yesaia 14:24
Nkɔmhyɛ A Etia AsiriaAsafo Awurade aka ntam se,“Nokware, sɛnea mahyehyɛ no, saa ara na ɛbɛyɛ,na sɛnea masusuw no, ɛrensesa.
/// Yesaia 14:25
Mɛsɛe Asiria pasaa wɔ mʼasase so;metiatia no so me mmepɔw so.Obeyi ne konnua afi me nkurɔfo so,na nʼadesoa nso afi wɔn so.”
/// Yesaia 14:26
Eyi ne nhyehyɛe a wɔayɛ ama wiase nyinaa;eyi ne nsa a wɔateɛ wɔ aman nyinaa so.
/// Yesaia 14:27
Na sɛ Asafo Awurade ayɛ nʼadwene a hena na obesiw no kwan?Na sɛ wateɛ ne nsa, hena na obesianka no?
/// Yesaia 14:28
Nkɔmhyɛ A Etia FilistifoSaa adehu yi baa afe a ɔhene Ahas wui no:
/// Yesaia 14:29
Munnni ahurusi, mo Filistifo,sɛ abaa a ɛbɔɔ mo no mu abubu;saa ɔwɔ no ase na ahurutoa befi aba,na nʼaba bɛyɛ ɔwɔ a ne ho yɛ hare na nʼano wɔ bɔre.
/// Yesaia 14:30
Ahiafo mu hiani benya didibea,na mmɔborɔni bɛda asomdwoe mu.Nanso wʼasefo de, mede ɔkɔm bɛtɔre wɔn ase;ebekunkum wo nkae no.
/// Yesaia 14:31
Twa adwo, kurow pon! Su teɛ mu kuropɔn!munguan, mo Filistifo!Wusiw kumɔnn bi fi atifi fam reba,na obiara ntwentwɛn ne nan ase wɔ wɔn mu.
/// Yesaia 14:32
Mmuae bɛn na wɔde bɛmasaa ɔman no ananmusifo?“Awurade de Sion atim hɔ,ne mu na nʼamanehunufo benya guankɔbea.”

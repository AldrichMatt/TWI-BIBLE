/// Nnwom 5:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Awurade, yɛ aso ma me nsɛmna dwen mʼapinisi ho.
/// Nnwom 5:2
Tie me mmoa ho sufrɛ,me Hene ne me Nyankopɔn,efisɛ wo na mebɔ wo mpae.
/// Nnwom 5:3
Awurade, wote me nne anɔpa;anɔpa, mede mʼadesrɛ ba wʼanimna mede anidaso twɛn.
/// Nnwom 5:4
Wonyɛ Onyankopɔn a wʼani gye bɔne ho;na amumɔyɛfo rentumi ne wo ntena.
/// Nnwom 5:5
Ahantanfo rentumi nnyina wʼanim;wukyi wɔn a wɔyɛ bɔne nyinaa.
/// Nnwom 5:6
Wosɛe wɔn a wɔka atosɛm;Awurade kyimogyapɛfo ne asisifo.
/// Nnwom 5:7
Nanso me, wo mmɔborɔhunu bebrebe nti,mɛba wo fi;na nidi mu na mɛkotowakyerɛ wʼasɔredan kronkron no.
/// Nnwom 5:8
Awurade kyerɛ me kwanwɔ wo trenee mumʼatamfo nti,bɔ wo kwan tee wɔ mʼanim.
/// Nnwom 5:9
Wɔn anom asɛm baako mpo nyɛ nokware;ɔsɛe ahyɛ wɔn koma ma.Wɔn menewa yɛ ɔda a so abuewɔde wɔn tɛkrɛma ka nnaadaasɛm.
/// Nnwom 5:10
Onyankopɔn, bu wɔn fɔ!Ma wɔn pɔw bɔne nyɛ wɔn asehwe.Pam wɔn; esiane wɔn bɔne dodow no nti,efisɛ wɔasɔre atia wo.
/// Nnwom 5:11
Nanso ma wɔn a woguan ba wo nkyɛn no ani nnye;ma wɔmfa anigye nto dwom daa.Trɛw wo bammɔ mu wɔ wɔn so,na wɔn a wɔdɔ wo din noma wɔn ani nnye wɔ wo mu.
/// Nnwom 5:12
Awurade, ampa ara, wuhyira atreneefo;na wode wʼayamye twa wɔn ho hyia sɛ nkatabo.

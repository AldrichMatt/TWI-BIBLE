/// Nnwom 61:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Ao, Onyankopɔn, tie me sufrɛ;tie me mpaebɔ.
/// Nnwom 61:2
Mifi asase ano frɛ wo,mefrɛ wo, efisɛ me koma atɔ beraw;fa me kɔ ɔbotan a ɛware sen me no so.
/// Nnwom 61:3
Woayɛ me guankɔbea,aban a ɛyɛ den ma ɔtamfo no.
/// Nnwom 61:4
Me kɔn dɔ sɛ metena wo ntamadan no mu afebɔɔna manya guankɔbea wɔ wo ntaban nwini ase.
/// Nnwom 61:5
Woate me bɔ a mehyɛe no, Onyankopɔn;na wode wɔn a wosuro wo din no agyapade ama me.
/// Nnwom 61:6
To ɔhene nkwanna mu bebree;na ne mfe mfi awo ntoatoaso nkodu awo ntoatoaso.
/// Nnwom 61:7
Ma onni hene wɔ Onyankopɔn anim daafa wʼadɔe ne wo nokware bɔ ne ho ban.
/// Nnwom 61:8
Afei mɛto ayeyi dwom ama wo din daana madi me bɔhyɛ so daa nyinaa.

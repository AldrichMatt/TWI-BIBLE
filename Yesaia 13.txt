/// Yesaia 13:1
Nkɔmhyɛ A Etia BabiloniaAmos babarima Yesaia adehu a ɛfa Babilonia ho ni:
/// Yesaia 13:2
Ma frankaa so wɔ bepɔw a hwee nni so no atifi,teɛ mu frɛ wɔn:nyama wɔnma wɔnhyɛn atitiriw no apon mu.
/// Yesaia 13:3
Mahyɛ mʼahotewfo;mafrɛ mʼakofo sɛ wɔnna mʼabufuwhyew adi,wɔn a wodi me nkonimdi ho ahurusi.
/// Yesaia 13:4
Tie huuyɛ bi a ɛwɔ mmepɔw no sote sɛ dɔm kɛse bi de!Tie akasakasa a ɛwɔ ahenni ahorow mu,te sɛ amanaman a wɔreboaboa wɔn ho ano!Asafo Awurade reboa dɔm ano akɔ ɔko.
/// Yesaia 13:5
Wofifi akyirikyiri aman so,wofifi ɔsoro ahye so,Awurade ne nʼakode a ɛyɛ nʼabufuwhyew;bɛsɛe ɔman no nyinaa.
/// Yesaia 13:6
Twa adwo, na Awurade da no abɛn;ɛbɛba te sɛ ɔsɛe a efi Otumfo no.
/// Yesaia 13:7
Eyi nti, nsa nyinaa bedwudwo,obiara koma bɛbotow.
/// Yesaia 13:8
Wɔbɛbɔ hu,ɔyaw ne apinisi bekita wɔn;wobebubu wɔn mu te sɛ ɔbea a awo aka no.Wɔde ahodwiriw bɛhwɛ wɔn ho wɔn ho anim,a wɔn ani asosɔ gya.
/// Yesaia 13:9
Hwɛ, Awurade da no rebada bɔne a abufuw ne abufuwhyew wɔ mu,ɛrebɛsɛe asase nona asɛe abɔnefo a wɔte so no.
/// Yesaia 13:10
Ɔsoro nsoromma ne wɔn akuw renhyerɛn.Sum beduru owia a ɛrepuena ɔsram nso renhyerɛn.
/// Yesaia 13:11
Mɛtwe wiase aso, ne bɔne nti,ne amumɔyɛfo wɔ wɔn nnebɔne ho.Metwa ahantanfo ahomaso somɛbrɛ nea odi awurukasɛm no ahantan ase.
/// Yesaia 13:12
Mɛma onipa ho ayɛ na asen sika kɔkɔɔ ankasaɔbɛsen Ofir sikakɔkɔɔ.
/// Yesaia 13:13
Enti mɛma ɔsoro awosow;na asase ahinhim wɔ ne sibeawɔ Asafo Awurade abufuwhyew anonʼabufuwhyewda no.
/// Yesaia 13:14
Te sɛ ɔwansan a ɔbɔmmɔfo rehwehwɛ no akum no,te sɛ nguan a wonni ɔhwɛfo,obiara bɛkɔ ne nkurɔfo mu,obiara beguan akɔ nʼasase so.
/// Yesaia 13:15
Obiara a wɔbɛfa no dedua no, wobehwirew no afoa akum no;wɔn a wɔbɛkyere wɔn no nyinaa bɛtotɔ wɔ afoa ano.
/// Yesaia 13:16
Wɔbɛtotow nkokoaa ahwehwe fam wɔ wɔn anim:wɔbɛfom wɔn afi mu nneɛma na wɔato wɔn yerenom mmonnaa.
/// Yesaia 13:17
Hwɛ mɛhwanyan Mediafo atia wɔn,wɔn na wɔmfa dwetɛ nyɛ hweena wɔn ani nnye sikakɔkɔɔ ho.
/// Yesaia 13:18
Wɔn tadua bɛbobɔ mmerante ahwehwe fam;wɔrenhu nkokoaa mmɔbɔna wɔrennya ayamhyehye mma mmofra.
/// Yesaia 13:19
Babilonia, ahenni ahorow mu ɔbohemaa,anuonyam a Babiloniafo de hoahoa wɔn ho noOnyankopɔn bɛdan abutuwte sɛ Sodom ne Gomora.
/// Yesaia 13:20
Nnipa rentena hɔ bioawo ntoatoaso nyinaa mu;Arabni biara rensi ne ntamadan wɔ hɔoguanhwɛfo biara remfa ne nguan nkɔhome wɔ hɔ.
/// Yesaia 13:21
Nanso sare so mmoa bɛda hɔ.Sakraman bɛyɛ nʼafi no mu ma;hɔ na mpatu bɛtena,hɔ na wura mu mmirekyi nso bedi agoru.
/// Yesaia 13:22
Mpataku bɛtena nʼabandennen mu,adompo bɛyɛ nʼahemfi fɛfɛ no mu ma.Ne bere aso,na wɔrentwe ne nna mu.

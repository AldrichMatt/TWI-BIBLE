/// Yesaia 24:1
Asase Sɛe A Efi AwuradeHwɛ, Awurade rebɛma asase ada mpanna wasɛe no;ɔbɛsɛe asase anina wabɔ sotefo ahwete,
/// Yesaia 24:2
ɛbɛyɛ saa araama ɔsɔfo sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama nnipa,ama owura sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama asomfo,ama awuraa sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama afenaa,ama nea ɔtɔn sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama nea ɔtɔ,ama nea ogye bosea sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama nea ɔma bosea,ama ɔkafo sɛnea ɛbɛyɛ ama nea ɔde ma.
/// Yesaia 24:3
Asase bɛda mpan koraa,na wɔafom so nneɛma.Awurade, na waka saa asɛm yi.
/// Yesaia 24:4
Asase so yɛ wosee, na ekisawiase kɔ ahoyeraw mu, na ekisa,nea wɔama no so wɔ asase so ho yeraw no.
/// Yesaia 24:5
Asase so nnipa agu ne ho fi;wɔanni mmara no so,wɔabu ahyɛde no sona wɔasɛe apam a ɛwɔ hɔ daa no.
/// Yesaia 24:6
Enti nnome amene asase noɛsɛ sɛ so nnipa nya wɔn afɔdi so akatuane saa nti wɔahyew asase no so nnipa,na kakraa bi pɛ na wɔaka.
/// Yesaia 24:7
Nsa foforo no ho yow na bobe no guan;wɔn a wogye wɔn ani no si apini.
/// Yesaia 24:8
Akasaebɔ mu anigye no agyae,ahosɛpɛwfo no nteɛteɛmu no agyae.Sankuten so nnwom dɛdɛ no ayɛ dinn.
/// Yesaia 24:9
Wɔnnto dwom mfa nnom nsa bio;mmorɔsa ayɛ nwen ama anomfo.
/// Yesaia 24:10
Kuropɔn a wɔasɛe no ada mpan;wɔatoto ofi biara apon mu.
/// Yesaia 24:11
Wɔpere nsa wɔ mmɔnten so;ahosɛpɛw nyinaa adan bosooyɛ,anigye nyinaa atu ayera wɔ asase no so.
/// Yesaia 24:12
Kuropɔn no asɛe,wɔabubu nʼapon mu nketenkete.
/// Yesaia 24:13
Saa ara na ɛbɛyɛ wɔ asase no sone amanaman no mu,sɛnea wɔboro ngodua a ɛyɛ no,anaa sɛnea wogyaw mpɛpɛ wɔ bobe twabere akyi no.
/// Yesaia 24:14
Wɔma wɔn nne so, wɔde anigye teɛteɛ mu;efi atɔe fam, wɔkamfo Awurade kɛseyɛ.
/// Yesaia 24:15
Enti apuei fam, monhyɛ Awurade anuonyam;momma Awurade din so, Israel Nyankopɔn no,wɔ supɔw a ɛwɔ po so no so.
/// Yesaia 24:16
Yɛte nnwonto fi asase ano yɛte se,“Anuonyam nka Ɔtreneeni no.”Nanso mekae se, “Matɔ beraw, mapa abaw!Minnue!Ɔfatwafo di huammɔ!Ɔnam nnaadaa so di huammɔ.”
/// Yesaia 24:17
Ehu, amoa ne afiri retwɛn mo,nnipa a mote asase so.
/// Yesaia 24:18
Nea ehu nti oguan nobɛtɔ amoa mu;nea ɔforo fi amoa mu noafiri beyi no.Wɔabue nsu apon wɔ ɔsoro,na asase nnyinaso wosow.
/// Yesaia 24:19
Wɔadwiriw asase nona emu apaapae,na asase awosow ankasa.
/// Yesaia 24:20
Asase tɔ ntintan te sɛ ɔsabowfo.Ehinhim biribiri te sɛ ntamadan wɔ mframa ano;nʼatuatew ho afɔdi ayɛ adesoa ama nona ɛhwe ase a ɛrensɔre bio.
/// Yesaia 24:21
Saa da no, Awurade bɛtweɔsoro atumfone ahemfo a wɔwɔ asase so aso.
/// Yesaia 24:22
Wɔbɛka wɔn nyinaa abɔ musɛ nneduafo ahyɛ afiase amoa mu;wɔbɛto wɔn mu wɔ afiasena nna bebree akyi no wɔatwe wɔn aso.
/// Yesaia 24:23
Ɔsram anim begu ase na owia ani awu;na Asafo Awurade bedi henewɔ Sion bepɔw so ne Yerusalem,ne wɔn mpanyimfo anim, wɔ anuonyam mu.

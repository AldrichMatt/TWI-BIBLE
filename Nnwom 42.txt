/// Nnwom 42:1
 NHOMA A ƐTO SO ABIENNnwom –Dwom Koramma “maskil” dwom.Sɛnea ɔforote pere hwehwɛ nsuwansuwa no,saa ara na me kra pere hwehwɛ wo, me Nyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 42:2
Onyankopɔn ho sukɔm de me kra, Onyankopɔn teasefo no.Da bɛn na metumi akohyia Onyankopɔn?
/// Nnwom 42:3
Me nisu ayɛ mʼaduanawia ne anadwo,na da mu nyinaa nnipa bisa me se:“Wo Nyankopɔn wɔ he?”
/// Nnwom 42:4
Saa nneɛma yi na mekaebere a mereka me koma mu nsɛm;sɛnea na midi asafokuw anim,na yɛde anigye nteɛteɛmu ne aseda nnwomnantew yuu bɔ mu kɔ Onyankopɔn fiwɔ afahyɛ da no.
/// Nnwom 42:5
Me kra, adɛn, na woabotow?Adɛn na woayɛ basaa wɔ me mu saa?Fa wo ho to Onyankopɔn so,na mɛkɔ so ayi no ayɛ,mʼAgyenkwa ne me Nyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 42:6
Me kra abotow wɔ me mu,enti mɛkae wofi Yordan asase so,Hermon mmepɔw so, fi Bepɔw Misar so.
/// Nnwom 42:7
Ebun kɔ bun muwɔ wo nsu a ɛworo gu no nnyigyei mu;wʼasorɔkye nyinaaabu afa me so.
/// Nnwom 42:8
Adekyee mu, Awurade kyerɛ nʼadɔe,adesae mu, ne dwom ba me nkyɛn,mpae a mebɔ srɛ me nkwa Nyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 42:9
Mibisa Onyankopɔn, me Botan no se,“Adɛn nti na wo werɛ afi me?Adɛn nti na ɛsɛ sɛ menantenantew twa adwo,mʼatamfo nhyɛso nti?”
/// Nnwom 42:10
Me nnompe te ɔdesani yawdi,mʼahohiahiafo ateetee nti,daa nyinaa wobisa me se,“Wo Nyankopɔn wɔ he?”
/// Nnwom 42:11
Me kra, adɛn, na woabotow?Adɛn na woayɛ basaa wɔ me mu saa?Fa wo ho to Onyankopɔn so,na mɛkɔ so ayi no ayɛ,mʼAgyenkwa ne me Nyankopɔn.

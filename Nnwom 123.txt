/// Nnwom 123:1
Dwom Ɔsoroforo dwom.Mema mʼani so kyerɛ wo,wo a wʼahengua si ɔsoro.
/// Nnwom 123:2
Sɛnea nkoa ani hwɛ wɔn wura nsa no,sɛnea afenaa ani hwɛ nʼawuraa nsa no no,saa ara na yɛn ani hwɛ Awurade, yɛn Nyankopɔnkosi sɛ obehu yɛn mmɔbɔ.
/// Nnwom 123:3
Hu yɛn mmɔbɔ, Awurade, hu yɛn mmɔbɔ,na wɔabu yɛn animtiaa bebree.
/// Nnwom 123:4
Ahomasofo fɛwdi ama yɛabrɛ dodo,ahantanfo animtiaabu afono yɛn.

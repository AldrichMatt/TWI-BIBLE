/// Nnwom 142:1
Dwom Dawid de. Bere a na ɔwɔ ɔbodan mu no. Mpaebɔ.Meteɛ mu su frɛ Awurade;mema me nne so su frɛ Awurade sɛ onhu me mmɔbɔ.
/// Nnwom 142:2
Mihwie mʼahiasɛm gu nʼanim;meka me haw kyerɛ no.
/// Nnwom 142:3
Sɛ me kra tɔ beraw wɔ me mu a,ɛyɛ wo na wunim me kwan.Ɔkwan a menam so nonnipa asum me afiri.
/// Nnwom 142:4
Hwɛ na hu, obiara nni me nifa so;mʼasɛm mfa obiara ho;minni guankɔbea bi;me nkwa mfa obiara ho.
/// Nnwom 142:5
Awurade, misu frɛ wo;mise, “Wone me guankɔbea,me kyɛfa wɔ ateasefo asase so.”
/// Nnwom 142:6
Tie me sufrɛ,na mewɔ ahohia kɛse mu;gye me fi mʼataafo nsam,efisɛ wɔn ho yɛ den sen me.
/// Nnwom 142:7
Yi me fi mʼafiase,na makamfo wo din.Afei atreneefo betwa me ho ahyiaesiane wo papa a woayɛ me no nti.

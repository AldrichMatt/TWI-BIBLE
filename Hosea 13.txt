/// Hosea 13:1
Awurade Abufuw A Etia IsraelSɛ Efraim kasa a, na nnipa ho popo;wɔma no so wɔ Israelman mu.Nanso Baalsom nti, onyaa afɔbu na owui.
/// Hosea 13:2
Afei wɔkɔ so yɛ bɔne ara;wɔde wɔn dwetɛ yɛ ahoni ma wɔn ho,nsɛsode no nyinaa no.Adwumfo no nam nyansakwan so na ayeyɛ.Wɔka nnipa yi ho asɛm se,“Wɔde nnipa bɔ afɔrena wofew nantwimma ahoni ano.”
/// Hosea 13:3
Ɛno nti, wɔbɛyɛ sɛ anɔpa omununkumsɛ anɔpa bosu a etu yera,sɛ ntɛtɛ a ehuw fi awiporowbeasɛ wusiw a ɛfa mfɛnsere mu kɔ.
/// Hosea 13:4
“Nanso mene Awurade wo Nyankopɔn,a miyii wo fii Misraim.Nnye Onyankopɔn biara nto mu sɛ me,anaa Ogyefo biara sɛ me.
/// Hosea 13:5
Mehwɛɛ wo wɔ nweatam so,asase a so yɛ hyew yiye no so.
/// Hosea 13:6
Memaa wɔn aduan no, wodi mee;wɔmee no, wɔyɛɛ ahantan;na wɔn werɛ fii me.
/// Hosea 13:7
Enti mɛtow ahyɛ wɔn so sɛ gyata,mɛtɛw wɔn wɔ akwan ho sɛ ɔsebɔ.
/// Hosea 13:8
Mɛtow ahyɛ wɔn so na matetew wɔn mute sɛ sisi bi a wɔawia ne mma.Mɛwe wɔn te sɛ gyata;wuram aboa besunsuan wɔn mu.
/// Hosea 13:9
“Wɔasɛe wo, Israel,efisɛ woasɔre atia me,me a meyɛ wo boafo.
/// Hosea 13:10
Wo hene wɔ he na wabegye wo?Wo sodifo a wɔwɔ wo nkurow nyinaa so no wɔ he,wɔn a wokaa faa wɔn ho se,‘Ma me ɔhene ne ahenemma’ no?
/// Hosea 13:11
Enti mʼabufuw mu, memaa wo ɔhene,na mʼanibere mu no, miyii no hɔ.
/// Hosea 13:12
Efraim afɔdi no wɔaboaboa anowɔakyerɛw ne bɔne agu hɔ.
/// Hosea 13:13
Ɔbɛte ɔyaw te sɛ ɔbea a ɔreko awo,na abofra no nnim nyansa nti;sɛ bere no du a,ɔmma awotwaa no ano.
/// Hosea 13:14
“Mɛpata agye wɔn afi ɔda tumi mu.Megye wɔn afi owu nsam.Owu, wo nwowɔe wɔ he?Asaman, wo sɛe wɔ he?“Merennya ayamhyehye biara,
/// Hosea 13:15
mpo sɛ ɔyɛ yiye wɔ ne nuanom mu a.Apuei mframa a efi Awurade hɔ bɛba,ɛbɛbɔ afi nweatam so;osu rentɔ asusowberena nʼabura bɛyow.Wɔbɛfom ne koradan mu nneɛmanea ɛsom bo no nyinaa.
/// Hosea 13:16
Ɛsɛ sɛ nnipa a wɔwɔ Samaria gye wɔn afɔdi to mu,efisɛ wɔayɛ dɔm atia wɔn Nyankopɔn.Wɔbɛtotɔ wɔ afoa ano;wɔbɛtotow wɔn mmofra ahwehwe fam,wɔn mmaa a wɔanyinsɛn nso wɔbɛpaapae wɔn mu.”

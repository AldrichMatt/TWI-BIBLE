/// Nnwom 6:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Awurade nka mʼanim wɔ wʼabufuw mu,na ntwe mʼaso nso wɔ wʼabufuwhyew mu.
/// Nnwom 6:2
Hu me mmɔbɔ, Awurade, efisɛ meyɛ mmrɛw;Awurade sa me yarena me nnompe wɔ ahoyeraw mu.
/// Nnwom 6:3
Me kra wɔ ahohia mu.Enkosi da bɛn, Ao Awurade, enkosi da bɛn?
/// Nnwom 6:4
San bra, Awurade, na begye me nkwa;gye me, wo dɔ a enni nhuammɔ no nti.
/// Nnwom 6:5
Obi a wawu no renkae wo da,hena na ofi ɔda mu kamfo wo?
/// Nnwom 6:6
Apinisi ama mayɛ mmerɛw.Anadwo mu nyinaa mede agyaadwotwa fɔw me mpa,na me nusu fɔw me sumii.
/// Nnwom 6:7
Awerɛhow ama mʼani so ayɛ me kusuu.Ɛredum; mʼatamfo nyinaa nti.
/// Nnwom 6:8
Mo nnebɔneyɛfo, montwe mo ho mfi me ho!efisɛ Awurade ate me sufrɛ.
/// Nnwom 6:9
Awurade ate me mmɔborɔsu;Awurade tie me sufrɛ.
/// Nnwom 6:10
Mʼatamfo nyinaa anim begu asena wɔn ho adwiriw wɔn;na wɔapatuw de fɛre asan wɔn akyi.

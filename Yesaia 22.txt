/// Yesaia 22:1
Nkɔmhyɛ A Ɛfa Yerusalem HoNkɔmhyɛ a etia Anisoadehu Bon no:Dɛn na ɛhaw mo mprempren,a mo nyinaa aforo kɔ adan atifi yi,
/// Yesaia 22:2
kurow a basabasayɛ ahyɛ no ma,huuyɛ ne ahosɛpɛw kuropɔn?Ɛnyɛ afoa ano na wʼatɔfo hweree wɔn nkwa,na wɔanwuwu wɔ ɔko ano nso.
/// Yesaia 22:3
Mo akannifo nyinaa abɔ mu aguan;wɔamfa tadua ankyekye wɔn.Mo a wɔkyeree mo nyinaa wɔfaa mo nneduafomo a muguan wɔ bere a na atamfo no wɔ akyirikyiri.
/// Yesaia 22:4
Enti mekae se, “Mumfi me sona mintwa adwo.Monnkyekye me werɛwɔ me nkurɔfo sɛe ho.”
/// Yesaia 22:5
Awurade, Asafo Awurade wɔ da bima huuyɛ, ntiatiaso ne ehuwɔ Anisoadehu Bon no mu,da a wɔde bubu afasuna wɔde su kyerɛ mmepɔw.
/// Yesaia 22:6
Elam fa bɛmma noka ne nteaseɛnamkafo ne apɔnkɔ ho;Kir ama kyɛm no so.
/// Yesaia 22:7
Nteaseɛnam ayɛ mo bon fɛfɛ no ma,na wɔde apɔnkɔsotefo agyinagyina kuropɔn apon no ano.
/// Yesaia 22:8
Wɔayi Yuda ho bammɔ no.Na da no wode wʼani tooakode a ɛwɔ Kwae mu Ahemfi no so;
/// Yesaia 22:9
wuhuu sɛ Dawid Kuropɔn no,akwan bebree deda ne bammɔ muwoboaa nsu anowɔ Ase Abura mu.
/// Yesaia 22:10
Wokan adan a ɛwɔ Yerusalem nona wudwiriw afi de boaa ɔfasu no ahoɔden.
/// Yesaia 22:11
Woyɛɛ nsukorae wɔ afasu abien no ntammaa nsu a ɛwɔ Abura Dedaw mu no,nanso woanhwehwɛ Onipa ko a ɔyɛe,na woammu Onipa a ɔhyehyɛe dedaada no.
/// Yesaia 22:12
Awurade, Asafo Awurade,frɛɛ wo saa da nosɛ su na twa adwo,sɛ yi wo tinwi na fura atweaatam.
/// Yesaia 22:13
Nanso hwɛ, anigye ne ahosɛpɛw wɔ hɔ;wokunkum anantwi ne nguan;wɔwe nam na wɔbow nsa!Wɔka se, “Momma yennidi na yɛnnom,efisɛ, ɔkyena yebewu!”
/// Yesaia 22:14
Awurade, Asafo Awurade ayi me asotiw se, “Wɔremfa saa bɔne yi nkyɛ wo da kosi wo wuda,” sɛnea Awurade, Asafo Awurade se ni.
/// Yesaia 22:15
Sɛɛ na Awurade, Asafo Awurade se,“Kɔ, kɔka kyerɛ saa ɔsomfo yi,Sebna a, ɔhwɛ ahemfi no so se,
/// Yesaia 22:16
Dɛn na woreyɛ wɔ ha na hena na ɔmaa wo kwansɛ bɔ wo da wɔ ha,worebɔ wo da wɔ baabi a ɛkorɔnna woretwa wo homebea wɔ ɔbotan mu?
/// Yesaia 22:17
“Hwɛ yiye, na Awurade rebeso wo mu denatow wo akyene, wo ɔhoɔdenfo.
/// Yesaia 22:18
Ɔbɛbobɔw wo, ama woayɛ kurukuruwa,na watow wo akyene ɔman kɛse bi sohɔ na wubewuna wo teaseɛnam fɛfɛ no aka hɔ,wo a woyɛ ahohora ma wo wura fi!
/// Yesaia 22:19
Megye wʼadwuma afi wo nsa muna wɔayi wo afi wo dibea so.
/// Yesaia 22:20
“Saa da no, mɛfrɛ me somfo Eliakim a ɔyɛ Hilkia babarima. 
/// Yesaia 22:21
Mede wʼatade yuu bɛhyɛ no na mede wʼabɔso abebare nʼasen mu na mede wo tumi ahyɛ ne nsa. Ɔbɛyɛ agya ama wɔn a wɔtete Yerusalem ne Yuda fi. 
/// Yesaia 22:22
Mede Dawid fi safe bɛma no; nea obebue no obiara rentumi nto mu; na nea ɔbɛto mu nso a obiara rentumi mmue. 
/// Yesaia 22:23
Mɛbɔ ne so sɛ pɛe ama watim, ɔbɛyɛ anuonyam agua ama nʼagya fi. 
/// Yesaia 22:24
Nʼabusua anuonyam nyinaa begyina ne so: nʼasefo ne ne nenanom, ne nkuku ne nkaka, fi nsanka, mprɛte so kosi nhina nyinaa so.
/// Yesaia 22:25
“Saa da no,” sɛnea Asafo Awurade se ni, “pɛe a wɔabɔ so ama atim no, betu ahwe fam na nʼadesoa no nso asɛe, Awurade, akasa!”

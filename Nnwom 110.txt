/// Nnwom 110:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Awurade ka kyerɛɛ me Wura se,“Tena me nifa sokosi sɛ mede wʼatamfobɛyɛ wʼanan ase ntiaso.”
/// Nnwom 110:2
Awurade bɛterɛw wʼahempema dennen mu afi Sion;na wubedi hene wɔ wʼatamfo so.
/// Nnwom 110:3
Wʼasraafo beyi wɔn koma muda a wobɛkɔ ɔsa.Tumi kronkron bɛyɛ wʼaduradena wo mmabun bɛba wo nkyɛnsɛnea obosu gugu anɔpahema.
/// Nnwom 110:4
Awurade aka ntamna ɔrensesa nʼadwene:“Woyɛ daapem sɔfosɛ Melkisedek sɛso.”
/// Nnwom 110:5
Awurade wɔ wo nifa so;ɔbɛdwerɛw ahemfo wɔ nʼabufuwhyew da no.
/// Nnwom 110:6
Obebu amanaman atɛn aboaboa awufo ano;na wadwerɛw asodifo a wɔwɔ asase so nyinaa.
/// Nnwom 110:7
Ɔbɛnom asuwa a ɛda ɔkwankyɛn mu nsuenti obedi nkonim.Ɔhene no bɛnom asuwa a ɛda ɔkwankyɛn mu nsu,na wanya ahoɔden, na wagyina nkonimdi mu.

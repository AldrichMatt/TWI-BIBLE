/// Nnwom 3:1
Dwom Dawid dwom a ɔtoe bere a ɔreguan afi ne babarima Absalom anim.Awurade, hwɛ sɛnea mʼatamfo dɔɔso fa!dodow no ara na wɔsɔre tia me!
/// Nnwom 3:2
Bebree no ara ka fa me ho se,“Onyankopɔn rennye no.”
/// Nnwom 3:3
Nanso wo, Awurade, woyɛ nkatabo a atwa me ho ahyiamʼanuonyam, nea Ɔpagyaw me ti.
/// Nnwom 3:4
Meteɛ mu su frɛ Awurade,na ogye me so fi ne bepɔw kronkron no so.
/// Nnwom 3:5
Metɔ hɔ na meda a,minyan bio, efisɛ Awurade ma migyina.
/// Nnwom 3:6
Merensuro atamfo mpem mpem awɔatwa me ho ahyia no.
/// Nnwom 3:7
Sɔre, Awurade!Gye me, me Nyankopɔn!Bobɔ mʼatamfo nyinaa abogye;na tutu amumɔyɛfo se.
/// Nnwom 3:8
Awurade hɔ na ogye fi ba.Ma wo nhyira mmra wo nkurɔfo so.

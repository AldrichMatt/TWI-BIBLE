/// Nnwom 84:1
Dwom Koramma dwom.Awurade Asafo,wʼatenae hɔ yɛ ahomeka!
/// Nnwom 84:2
Me kra pere, mpo ɛtɔ beraw,sɛ ɔbɛkɔ Awurade adiwo;me koma ne me honam teɛ mufrɛ Onyankopɔn teasefo.
/// Nnwom 84:3
Mpo akasanoma anya ofi,na asomfena nso anwen ne berebuw,faako a obesow ne mmawɔ beae bi a ɛbɛn wʼafɔremuka,Awurade Asafo, me Hene ne me Nyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 84:4
Nhyira nka wɔn a wɔte wo fi;na wɔkamfo wo din daa.
/// Nnwom 84:5
Nhyira nka wɔn a wɔwɔ wo mu ahoɔden,na wɔde wɔn koma fi akwantu ase kɔhwehwɛ wo.
/// Nnwom 84:6
Sɛ wɔfa Baka Bon no mu awɔma hɔ yɛ nsuwansuwa;asusow bere nsu so taataa hɔ.
/// Nnwom 84:7
Wɔkɔ no ahoɔden so bere nyinaa,kosi sɛ wɔn mu biara bepue Onyankopɔn anim wɔ Sion.
/// Nnwom 84:8
Asafo Awurade Nyankopɔn, tie me mpaebɔ;Yakob Nyankopɔn, yɛ aso ma me.
/// Nnwom 84:9
Ao, Onyankopɔn, hwɛ yɛn nkatabo;fa ahummɔbɔ hwɛ nea woasra no ngo no.
/// Nnwom 84:10
Wʼadiwo hɔ da koro tenaye sen nnafua apem wɔ baabi foforo;mepɛ sɛ mɛyɛ ɔponanohwɛfo wɔ Onyankopɔn fisen sɛ mɛtena amumɔyɛfo ntamadan mu.
/// Nnwom 84:11
Efisɛ Awurade Nyankopɔn yɛ owia ne kyɛm;Awurade hu mmɔbɔ na ɔhyɛ anuonyam;ɔmmfa ade pa biara nkamewɔn a wɔteɛ.
/// Nnwom 84:12
Asafo Awurade,nhyira nka onipa a ɔde ne ho to wo so.

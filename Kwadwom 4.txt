/// Kwadwom 4:1
Hwɛ sɛnea sikakɔkɔɔ ayera ne hyerɛn no niSikakɔkɔɔ ankasa na ho abiri saa!Wɔatoto aboɔdemmo kronkron apetewɔ abɔnten biara so.
/// Kwadwom 4:2
Hwɛ sɛnea Sion mmabarima a wodi muna anka wɔsom bo sɛ sikakɔkɔɔ no ayɛ,mprempren wɔfa wɔn sɛ dɔte nkuku,ɔnwemfo nsa ano adwuma!
/// Kwadwom 4:3
Mpo adompo yi wɔn nufuma wɔn mma num,nanso me nkurɔfo ayɛ atirimɔdenfosɛ sohori a ɔwɔ sare so.
/// Kwadwom 4:4
Osukɔm nti, akokoaa tɛkrɛmafam ne dodo mu;mmofra no srɛ aduan,nanso obiara mfa mma wɔn.
/// Kwadwom 4:5
Wɔn a na anka wodi nnuan pa noohia buruburoo aka wɔn wɔ mmɔnten so.Wɔn a wɔde sirikyi tetew wɔn nomprempren wɔdeda nso a wɔaboa ano so.
/// Kwadwom 4:6
Me nkurɔfo asotweso sen Sodom awotuu wɔn mpofirim no,a wɔannya ɔboafo biara de.
/// Kwadwom 4:7
Na wɔn ahenemma ho twa sen sukyerɛmma,na wɔnan sen nufusu,na wɔn nipadua no bere, na ɛwɔ ahoɔden te sɛ abohemaa,na wɔte sɛ hoabo.
/// Kwadwom 4:8
Nanso mprempren de, wobiri sen wusiw bɔtɔ;na wonhu wɔn yiye wɔ mmɔnten so.Wɔn were atwintwam wɔ wɔn nnompe so;ho awo wosee sɛ abaa.
/// Kwadwom 4:9
Wɔn a wɔtotɔɔ wɔ afoa ano no ye koraasen wɔn a ɔkɔm kunkum wɔn;ɔkɔm tware wɔn ma wowuwu,esiane aduan a enni mfuw so nti.
/// Kwadwom 4:10
Mmea a wɔwɔ ayamhyehyede wɔn nsa anoa wɔn ankasa mma,ayɛ wɔn aduan,bere a wɔsɛee me nkurɔfo no.
/// Kwadwom 4:11
Awurade ada nʼabufuwhyew nyinaa adi;wahwie nʼabufuw a ano yɛ den no.Ɔsɔɔ ogya ano wɔ Sionma ɛhyew ne fapem.
/// Kwadwom 4:12
Asase so ahemfo annye anni,wiase mu nnipa nso annye annisɛ atamfo betumi ahyɛnYerusalem apon mu.
/// Kwadwom 4:13
Nanso esii, nʼadiyifo no bɔnene nʼasɔfo amumɔyɛ nti,wɔn na wohwiee atreneefo mogyaguu wɔ kuropɔn no mu.
/// Kwadwom 4:14
Afei wɔkeka wɔ mmɔnten sote sɛ nnipa a wɔn ani afura.Mogya a ama wɔn ho agu fi no ntiobiara suro sɛ ɔde ne ho bɛka wɔn ntade.
/// Kwadwom 4:15
Nnipa teɛteɛɛ mu gu wɔn so se, “Mumfi ha nkɔ! Mo ho ntew!”“Monkɔ, monkɔ! Mommfa mo ho nka yɛn.”Sɛ woguan kɔnenam aamanaman no mu nnipa ka se,“Wɔrentumi ntena ha bio.”
/// Kwadwom 4:16
Awurade ankasa abɔ wɔn ahwete;nʼani nni wɔn so bio.Wɔmmfa nidi mma asɔfo,na wɔn ani nsɔ mpanyimfo.
/// Kwadwom 4:17
Nea ɛka ho bio, yɛn ani anni ammoa yɛn,yɛpɛɛ sɛ yebenya mmoa, nanso ɛyɛɛ ɔkwa;yefi yɛn abantenten so de yɛn ani tooɔman a wɔrentumi nnye yɛn so.
/// Kwadwom 4:18
Nnipa dii yɛn ntɛntɛnso wɔ yɛn anammɔntu mu,enti yɛantumi annantew yɛn mmɔnten so.Yɛn awiei bɛnee, na wɔkan yɛn nna,efisɛ na yɛn awiei adu so.
/// Kwadwom 4:19
Yɛn ataafo ho yɛ haresen akɔre a wɔwɔ wim;wɔtaa yɛn faa mmepɔw sona wɔtɛw yɛn wɔ sare so.
/// Kwadwom 4:20
Nea Awurade asra no ngo, yɛn nkwa home ankasa no,kɔtɔɔ wɔn mfiri mu.Yegye dii sɛ ne nwini ase,yebenya nkwa wɔ amanaman no mu.
/// Kwadwom 4:21
Di ahurusi na ma wʼani nnye, Edom Babea,wo a wote Us asase so.Wɔde kuruwa no bɛma wo nso bi;wobɛbow na wɔabɔ wo adagyaw.
/// Kwadwom 4:22
Ɔbabea Sion, wʼasotwe bɛba awiei;ɔremma wo nkɔ so ntena nnommum mu.Nanso Ɔbabea Edom, obetua wo bɔne so kana wada wʼamumɔyɛ adi.

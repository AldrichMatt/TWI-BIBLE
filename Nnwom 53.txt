/// Nnwom 53:1
Dwom Dawid dwom.Ɔkwasea ka wɔ ne koma mu se,“Onyankopɔn bi nni hɔ.”Porɔwee adi wɔn, na wɔn akwan yɛ animguase.Obiara nni hɔ a ɔyɛ ade pa.
/// Nnwom 53:2
Onyankopɔn fi ɔsoro hwɛ adesamma wɔ famsɛ obehu wɔn mu bi awɔwɔ ntease,anaa ebinom a wɔhwehwɛ Onyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 53:3
Nanso nnipa nyinaa atwe wɔn ho afi Onyankopɔn ho;wɔn nyinaa ayɛ bɔne.Obiara nni hɔ a ɔyɛ papa.Ɔbaako mpo nni hɔ.
/// Nnwom 53:4
Enti abɔnefo rensua nyansa ana?Wɔwe me nkurɔfo sɛnea nnipa di aduan.Wonnsu mfrɛ Onyankopɔn.
/// Nnwom 53:5
Wɔn na wɔwɔ hɔ a wɔabɔ huboawɔ faako a ehu nni.Onyankopɔn hwetee wɔn a wɔtow hyɛɛ wo so no nnompe mu;wuguu wɔn anim ase, efisɛ Onyankopɔn buu wɔn animtiaa.
/// Nnwom 53:6
Ao, Israel nkwagye mfi Sion mmra!Awurade bɛsan de ne nkurɔfo agyapade ama wɔn.Na Yakob adi ahurusi, na Israel ani agye!

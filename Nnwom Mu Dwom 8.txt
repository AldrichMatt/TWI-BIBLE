/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:1
Sɛ anka woyɛɛ me nuabarimaa wunum me na nufu ano,na mihuu wo wɔ abɔnten so a,anka mefew wʼano,a obiara rentumi mmu me animtiaa.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:2
Anka medi wʼanimna mede wo aba me na fi,ɔno a wakyerɛkyerɛ me.Anka mɛma wo bobesa a wɔde pɛprɛ afra mu,me ntunkum mu nsu dɔkɔdɔkɔ no bi.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:3
Ne nsa benkum da mʼatikona ne nsa nifa aka me afam ne bo.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:4
Yerusalem mmabea, mehyɛ mo sɛ,morennyan na morenhwanyan ɔdɔ mukosi bere a ɛsɛ mu.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:5
Nnamfonom:Hena na ofi sare so reba aotweri ne dɔfo koko mu yi?Ababaa:Mekanyan wo wɔ aprɛ dua no ase;ɛhɔ na wo na nyinsɛnee wo,ɛhɔ na nea ɔkoo awo no woo wo.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:6
Fa me to wo koma so sɛ nsɔwanode,sɛ nsɔwanode wɔ wʼabasa so,efisɛ ɔdɔ ano yɛ den sɛ owu,ne ninkutwe tim hɔ sɛ ɔda.Ɛhyehye sɛ ogyatannaa,sɛ ogyatannaa a ano yɛ den pa ara.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:7
Nsu dodow rentumi nnum ɔdɔ;nsubɔnten rentumi nhohoro nkɔ.Sɛ obi de ne fi ahonya nyinaa bɛsesa ɔdɔ aanka wɔremmu no ade a ɛsom bo.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:8
Nnamfonom:Yɛwɔ nuabea akumaa bi a,ne nufu mmobɔɔ ɛ.Dɛn na yɛbɛyɛ ama yɛn nuabea yiwɔ da a wobebisa no ase?
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:9
Sɛ ɔyɛ ɔfasu a,yebesi dwetɛ abantenten abɔ ne ho ban.Sɛ ɔyɛ ɔpon a,yɛde sida besiw ne pon ano kwan.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:10
Ababaa:Meyɛ ɔfasu,na me nufu te sɛ abantenten.Enti nʼani so no mayɛ sɛ obi aɔde nea ɛsɔ ani reba.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:11
Na Salomo wɔ bobeturo wɔ Baal-Hamon;ɔde ne bobeturo no maa apaafo.Na ɛsɛ sɛ wɔn mu biara tua nonnwetɛbona apem.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:12
Nanso me bobeturo yi yɛ me de.Me de, meremfa mma;Salomo de ne de gye nnwetɛbona apem,na otua nʼapaafo nnwetɛbona ahannu ma wɔhwɛ ne nnuaba no so.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:13
Aberante:Wo a wote turo no mune nnamfonom a wɔka wo ho,ma mente wo nne.
/// Nnwom Mu Dwom 8:14
Ababaa:Bra ma yɛnkɔ, me dɔfo,na yɛ wo ho sɛ ɔdabɔ baanaasɛ ɔforote ba aɔwɔ mmepɔw a nnuhuam ahyɛ so ma so.

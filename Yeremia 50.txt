/// Yeremia 50:1
Asɛm A Ɛfa Babilonia HoAsɛm a Awurade nam odiyifo Yeremia so ka faa Babilonia ne Babiloniafo asase ho ni:
/// Yeremia 50:2
“Mommɔ no dawuru na mompae mu nka wɔ amanaman mu.Momma frankaa so na mompae mu nka;na momma wɔnte sɛ,‘Wɔbɛfa Babilonia;wobegu Bel anim ase,na ehu bɛhyɛ Merodak ma.Wobegu ne nsɛsode anim asena ehu bɛhyɛ nʼahoni ma.’
/// Yeremia 50:3
Ɔman bi fi atifi fam bɛtow ahyɛ ne sona wɔbɛyɛ nʼasase amamfo.Obiara rentena so;nnipa ne mmoa beguan afi so.
/// Yeremia 50:4
“Nna no mu ne saa bere no,”Awurade na ose,“Israelfo ne Yudafo bɛka abɔ mude nusu akɔhwehwɛ wɔn Awurade, wɔn Nyankopɔn.
/// Yeremia 50:5
Wobebisa ɔkwan a ɛkɔ Sionna wɔde wɔn ani akyerɛ hɔ.Wɔbɛba de wɔn ho abɛbɔ Awuradewɔ daa apam awerɛ remfi mu.
/// Yeremia 50:6
“Me nkurɔfo ayɛ nguan a wɔayera;wɔn nguanhwɛfo ama wɔafom ɔkwanma wokyinkyin mmepɔw so.Wokyinkyin bepɔw ne koko sona wɔn werɛ fii wɔn ankasa homebea.
/// Yeremia 50:7
Obiara a ohuu wɔn no tetew wɔn mu:wɔn atamfo kae se, ‘Yenni fɔ,efisɛ wɔyɛɛ bɔne tiaa Awurade, wɔn nokware didibea no,Awurade wɔn agyanom anidaso no.’
/// Yeremia 50:8
“Munguan mfi Babilonia;mumfi Babiloniafo asase so,na monyɛ sɛ mpapo a wodi nguankuw anim.
/// Yeremia 50:9
Mɛkanyan amanaman akɛse nkabomafi atifi fam asase so de wɔn abɛko atia Babilonia.Wobegyinagyina wɔn afa atia no,wobefi atifi fam ako afa no.Wɔn agyan bɛyɛ sɛ dɔmmarima a wɔakwadaw akodi mu,wɔn a wɔmmfa nsapan nsan wɔn akyi.
/// Yeremia 50:10
Enti wɔbɛfow Babilonia;wɔn a wɔfow no nyinaa benya asade bebree,”Awurade na ose.
/// Yeremia 50:11
“Esiane sɛ mo ani gye na mudi ahurusi,mo a mofow mʼagyapade,sɛ muhuruhuruw anigye so te sɛ nantwiforo a ɔwɔ sare frɔmfrɔm muna musu te sɛ apɔnkɔnini nti,
/// Yeremia 50:12
wo na ani bewu pa ara;nea ɔwoo wo anim begu ase.Ɔbɛyɛ akumaa wɔ amanaman no mu,sare, asase wosee ne nweatam.
/// Yeremia 50:13
Awurade abufuw nti, obiara rentena hɔna ɛbɛda mpan korakora.Wɔn a wotwa mu wɔ Babilonia nyinaa ho bedwiriw wɔn na wɔadi ne ho fɛwesiane nʼapirakuru nyinaa nti.
/// Yeremia 50:14
“Munnyinagyina mo afa ntwa Babilonia ho nhyia,mo a motow agyan nyinaa.Montow no! Munnyaw bɛmma biara,efisɛ wayɛ bɔne atia Awurade.
/// Yeremia 50:15
Monteɛ mu ntia no wɔ afanan nyinaa!Ɔma ne nsa so, nʼabandennen bubu,wodwiriw nʼafasu gu fam.Esiane sɛ eyi yɛ Awurade aweretɔ no nti,montɔ ne so were;monyɛ no sɛnea wayɛ afoforo no.
/// Yeremia 50:16
Munyi ogufo mfi Babilonia,ɔne otwafo a okura ne kantankrankyi wɔ otwabere mu no.Esiane nhyɛsofo no afoa ntiobiara nsan nkɔ ne nkurɔfo mu,obiara nguan nkɔ nʼankasa asase so.
/// Yeremia 50:17
“Israel yɛ nguankuw a wɔahwetea gyata apam wɔn.Nea odii kan tetew ne muyɛ Asiriahene;na nea ɔdwerɛwee ne nnompe akyiri noyɛ Babiloniahene Nebukadnessar.”
/// Yeremia 50:18
Ɛno nti, sɛɛ na Asafo Awurade, Israel Nyankopɔn se:“Mɛtwe Babiloniahene ne nʼasase asosɛnea metwee Asiriahene aso no.
/// Yeremia 50:19
Na mede Israel bɛsan aba nʼankasa didibea,na obedidi wɔ Karmel ne Basan;ɔbɛmee wɔ Efraim ne Gilead nkoko so.
/// Yeremia 50:20
Saa nna no ne saa bere no,”Awurade na ose,“Wɔbɛhwehwɛ Israel afɔbu mu,nanso wɔrennya bi,wɔbɛhwehwɛ Yuda nnebɔne mu,nanso wɔrenhu bi,efisɛ, mede nkae a migyaa wɔn no bɔne bɛkyɛ wɔn.
/// Yeremia 50:21
“Montow nhyɛ Merataim asase sone nnipa a wɔte Pekod.Montaa wɔn, munkunkum wɔnna monsɛe wɔn korakora,”Awurade na ose.“Monyɛ biribiara a mahyɛ mo no.
/// Yeremia 50:22
Ɔko mu gyegyeegye wɔ asase no so,ɔsɛe kɛse nnyigyei!
/// Yeremia 50:23
Hwɛ sɛnea wɔabubu na wɔabobɔasase nyinaa so asae!Hwɛ sɛnea Babilonia ada mpanwɔ amanaman no mu!
/// Yeremia 50:24
Misum wo afiri, Babilonia,na eyii wo ansa na worehu;wohuu wo kyeree woefisɛ woko tiaa Awurade.
/// Yeremia 50:25
Otumfo Awurade abue nʼakode adekoradanna wayi nʼabufuwhyew akode de apue,efisɛ Asafo Awurade wɔ adwuma yɛwɔ Babiloniafo asase so.
/// Yeremia 50:26
Mumfi akyirikyiri mmra ne so.Mummubu nʼasan no ani;monhɔre no siw sɛ awi akuwakuw.Monsɛe no korakorana munnyaw nkae biara mma no.
/// Yeremia 50:27
Munkunkum nʼanatwinini nyinaa;momfa wɔn nkɔ mma wonkokum wɔn!Mmusu nka wɔn! Efisɛ wɔn da no adu,bere a wɔde bɛtwe wɔn aso no.
/// Yeremia 50:28
Muntie akobɔfo ne aguanfo a wofi Babilonia nosɛ wɔreka wɔ Sionsɛnea Awurade, yɛn Nyankopɔn atɔ were,nʼasɔrefi ho aweretɔ.
/// Yeremia 50:29
“Momfrɛ agyantowfo nkɔtoa Babilonia,wɔn a wɔtow agyan nyinaa.Muntwa ne ho nhyia;mommma obiara nguan.Muntua ne nneyɛe so ka;monyɛ no sɛnea wayɛ.Efisɛ wabu Awurade animtiaa,Israel kronkronni no.
/// Yeremia 50:30
Enti ne mmerante bɛtotɔ wɔ mmɔnten so;wɔbɛtɔre nʼasraafo nyinaa ase saa da no,”Awurade na ose.
/// Yeremia 50:31
“Hwɛ, mikyi wo; ɔhantanni.”Awurade, Asafo Awurade na ose,“efisɛ wo da no adubere a wɔde bɛtwe wʼaso no.
/// Yeremia 50:32
Ɔhantanni no behintiw na wahwe ase,obiara remmoa no mma ɔnnsɔre;mɛto ne nkurow mu gyana ahyew wɔn a atwa ne ho ahyia nyinaa.”
/// Yeremia 50:33
Sɛɛ na Asafo Awurade se:“Wɔhyɛ Israelfo so,na Yudafo nso saa ara.Wɔn nnommumfafo no kura wɔn dennena wɔmpɛ sɛ wogyaa wɔn ma wɔkɔ.
/// Yeremia 50:34
Nanso wɔn Gyefo yɛ ɔhoɔdenfo;ne din ne Asafo Awurade.Obedi wɔn asɛm ama wɔn anibere so,sɛnea ɔde ahotɔ bɛba wɔn asase no so,na wɔn a wɔte Babilonia no nea wahyɛ wɔn ahometew.
/// Yeremia 50:35
“Afoa a etia Babiloniafo!”Awurade na ose,“etia wɔn a wɔte Babiloniana etia nʼadwumayɛfo ne nʼanyansafo!
/// Yeremia 50:36
Afoa a etia nʼatoro adiyifo!Wɔbɛdan nkwaseafo.Afoa a etia nʼakofo!Ehu bɛhyɛ wɔn ma.
/// Yeremia 50:37
Afoa a etia nʼapɔnkɔ ne ne nteaseɛnamne ananafo a wɔwɔ wɔn mu nyinaa!Wɔbɛdan mmea.Afoa a etia nʼademude!Wɔbɛfom.
/// Yeremia 50:38
Ɔyow wɔ nʼasu ahorow so!Ɛbɛyoyow.Efisɛ ɛyɛ ahoni asase,ahoni a ehu bɛma wɔabobɔ adam.
/// Yeremia 50:39
“Enti sare so mmoa ne mpataku bɛtena hɔ,na ɛhɔ na patu bɛtena.Obiara rentena so bioefi awo ntoatoaso kosi awo ntoatoaso.
/// Yeremia 50:40
Sɛnea Onyankopɔn tuu Sodom ne Gomorane wɔn nkurow gui no,”Awurade na ose,“saa ara na obiara rentena hɔ;onipa biara rentena mu.
/// Yeremia 50:41
“Monhwɛ! Asraafo bi fi atifi fam reba;ɔman kɛse ne ahemfo bebree,wɔrehwanyan wɔn afi nsase ano.
/// Yeremia 50:42
Wokurakura bɛmma ne mpeaw;wɔn tirim yɛ den, na wonni ahummɔbɔ.Wɔn nnyigyei te sɛ po a ɛworo so.Sɛ wɔtete wɔn apɔnkɔ so a,wɔba sɛ mmarima a wɔrekɔ saabɛtow ahyɛ wo so, Ɔbabea Babilonia.
/// Yeremia 50:43
Babiloniahene ate wɔn ho nsɛm,ne nsa sesa ne ho kwa.Ahohiahia akyekyere noɔyaw a ɛte sɛ ɔbea a ɔrekyem wɔ awo so.
/// Yeremia 50:44
Sɛnea gyata a ofi Yordan nkyɛkyerɛ murekɔ adidibea frɔmfrɔm no,saa ara na mɛtaa Babilonia fi nʼasase so wɔ bere tiaa bi mu.Hena ne nea mayi no sɛ ɔnyɛ eyi?Hena na ɔte sɛ me, na hena na obetumi ne me adi asi?Na oguanhwɛfo bɛn na obetumi asɔre atia me?”
/// Yeremia 50:45
Enti tie nea Awurade ahyehyɛ atia Babilonia,nea wabɔ ne tirim sɛ ɔbɛyɛ de atia Babiloniafo asase ni:Nguankuw no mu nkumaa no wɔbɛtwe wɔn akɔ;wɔn nti ɔbɛsɛe adidibea no koraa.
/// Yeremia 50:46
Babilonia nnommumfa nnyigyei bɛma asase awosow;ne nteɛteɛmu gyegyeegye wɔ amanaman no mu.

/// Hesekiel 27:1
Tiro Ho KwadwomAwurade asɛm baa me nkyɛn se: 
/// Hesekiel 27:2
“Onipa ba, ma kwadwom a ɛfa Tiro ho so. 
/// Hesekiel 27:3
Ka kyerɛ Tiro a ɔda po abobow ano, na ɔne aguadifo bebree a wɔwɔ mpoano nkurow so di gua se, eyi ne asɛm a Otumfo Awurade ka:“ ‘Woka se, Tiro,“Me ho yɛ fɛ yiye.”
/// Hesekiel 27:4
Wo tumidi wɔ po so akyirikyiri;wʼadansifo maa wʼahoɔfɛ wiee pɛyɛ.
/// Hesekiel 27:5
Wo nnua a wɔpaee nyinaayɛ ɔpepaw a efi Senir.Wɔde Lebanon sidana ɛyɛɛ ahyɛn so nnua maa wo.
/// Hesekiel 27:6
Odum nnua a efi Basanna wɔde yɛɛ wʼatabon;Kwabɔhɔre nnua a efi Kipro mpoanona wɔde yɛɛ wʼahyɛn abrannaa, a wɔde asonse asɛw so.
/// Hesekiel 27:7
Misraim nwera papa bi a wɔadi mu adwinina wɔde yɛɛ wʼahyɛn so mframatama ɛyɛɛ wo frankaa nso;wʼapon ne mfɛnsere ano ntama yɛ abibitam ne beredum ntamaa efi Elisa mpoano.
/// Hesekiel 27:8
Sidonfo ne Arwadfo na wɔhare wʼahyɛn;wo mmarima anyansafo. Tiro,na wɔyɛ adwumayɛfo wɔ wʼahyɛn mu.
/// Hesekiel 27:9
Gebal adwumfo adedaw tenaa wʼahyɛn musɛ dua dwumfo a wotuatua ahyɛn no mu ntokuru.Po so ahyɛn ne wɔn hyɛnmufo nyinaabaa wo nkyɛn ne wo bedii nsesagua.
/// Hesekiel 27:10
“ ‘Persia, Lidia ne Put mmarimasomee sɛ asraafo wɔ wo nsraadɔm mu.Wɔde wɔn nkatabo ne dade kyɛwsensɛn wʼafasu ho,de hyɛɛ wo anuonyam.
/// Hesekiel 27:11
Arwad ne Helek mmarimawɛn wʼafasu ho nyinaaGammad mmarimatenaa wʼabantenten mu.Wɔde wɔn nkatabo sensɛn wʼafasu homaa wʼahoɔfɛ dii mu.
/// Hesekiel 27:12
“ ‘Wʼaguade a ɛma wonyaa wo ho bebree nti, Tarsis ne wo dii gua, wɔde dwetɛ, dade, sanyaa ne sumpii sesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:13
“ ‘Helafo, Tubalfo ne Mesekfo ne wo dii gua, wɔde nkoa ne kɔbere nneɛma sesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:14
“ ‘Bet Togarma mmarima de adwumayɛ apɔnkɔ, ɔsa apɔnkɔ ne mfurum mma bɛsesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:15
“ ‘Roda mmarima ne wo dii gua, na mpoano nkurow bebree yɛɛ wʼadetɔfo a wɔde asonse ne duabo nnua tuaa wo ka.
/// Hesekiel 27:16
“ ‘Aram ne wo dii gua esiane wo nneɛma bebrebe a woyɛ nti; wɔde nsrammabo, ntama a ɛberedum, nea wɔadi mu adwinni, nwera pa, nnɛnkyerema ne bota bɛsesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:17
“ ‘Yuda ne Israel ne wo dii gua; wɔde awi a efi Minit ne brodo, ɛwo, ngo ne ahyehyesrade sesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:18
“ ‘Damasko ne wo dii gua wɔ nsa a efi Helbon ne nguan ho nwi a efi Sahar mu, esiane nneɛma a woyɛ ne wʼaguade a ama woanya wo ho bebree no nti. 
/// Hesekiel 27:19
Danfo ne Helafo a wofi Usal tɔɔ wʼaguade na wɔde dade a wɔaboro, sesadua ne ahunuanyankwa sesaa wʼaguade.
/// Hesekiel 27:20
“ ‘Dedan de apɔnkɔnguasotam ne wo dii gua.
/// Hesekiel 27:21
“ ‘Arabfo ne Kedar mmapɔmma nyinaa yɛɛ wʼaguadifo a wɔde nguantenmma, adwennini ne mpapo ne wo dii gua.
/// Hesekiel 27:22
“ ‘Seba ne Raama aguadifo ne wo dii gua; wɔde nnuhuam ahorow a ɛte apɔw, aboɔden abo ne sikakɔkɔɔ ne wo bedii nsesagua.
/// Hesekiel 27:23
“ ‘Haran, Kan ne Eden, aguadifo a wofi Seba, Asur ne Kilmad ne wo dii gua. 
/// Hesekiel 27:24
Wo gua so hɔ, wɔde ntama a ɛyɛ fɛ, nnɛmmatam a ɛyɛ tuntum, adwinne ahorow ne ntama fɛfɛ a ɛwɔ ahosu ahorow a wɔde nhoma a wɔakyinkyim abobɔ no apɔwapɔw na ayɛ ne wo bedii gua.
/// Hesekiel 27:25
“ ‘Tarsis ahyɛn yɛ adwumasɛ asoafo ma wʼaguade.Adesoa duruduru ayɛ wo mawɔ po no mfimfini.
/// Hesekiel 27:26
Wʼaharefo no fa wode wo kɔ po so akyirikyirinanso apuei ahum bebubu wo mu nketenketewɔ po no mfimfini.
/// Hesekiel 27:27
Wʼahonya, aguade ne adetɔnnnewʼahyɛn akwankyerɛfo, wʼahyɛn mufo newɔn a wotuatua wʼahyɛn mu ntokuru,wʼaguadifo ne wʼasraafo nyinaa,ne obiara a ɔwɔ hyɛn no mubɛmem akɔ po ase tɔnn,da a wo hyɛn no bɛbɔ.
/// Hesekiel 27:28
Mpoano asase bɛwosowbere a wo hyɛn mufo no reteɛteɛ mu.
/// Hesekiel 27:29
Wɔn a wokurakura atabon no nyinaabegyaw wɔn ahyɛn hɔ;hyɛn akwankyerɛfo ne hyɛn mufo nyinaabegyinagyina mpoano.
/// Hesekiel 27:30
Wɔbɛma wɔn nne soasu osu yaayaw ama wo;wɔbɛpete mfutuma agu wɔn ti sona wɔayantayantam nsõ mu.
/// Hesekiel 27:31
Wo nti wɔbɛbobɔ tikwawna wɔafurafura atweaatam.Wɔde ɔkra mu ahoyerawne awerɛhow a mu yɛ denbegyam wo.
/// Hesekiel 27:32
Bere a wɔretwa adwo redi awerɛhow nowɔbɛma kwadwom bi a ɛfa wo ho so se:“Hena na wama no atɔ mumte sɛ Tiro a po atwa ne ho ahyia yi?”
/// Hesekiel 27:33
Bere a wʼaguade kɔɔ po ahorow so nowoboaa aman bebree;Wʼahonyade ne wʼaguademaa asase so ahemfo nyaa wɔn ho.
/// Hesekiel 27:34
Afei po abubu wo mu nketenketewɔ asu no bun muwʼaguade ne wʼadwumayɛfo nyinaane wo amem kɔ asu ase.
/// Hesekiel 27:35
Wɔn a wɔtete mpoano nyinaaho adwiriw wɔn wɔ wo ho;ehu ma wɔn ahemfo ho popona wɔn anim sinsiam.
/// Hesekiel 27:36
Aman no mu aguadifo bɔ ntwɔm gu wo sowoaba awiei a ɛyɛ huna wɔrenhu wo bio.’ ”

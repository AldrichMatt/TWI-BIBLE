/// Yesaia 30:1
Munnue, Ɔman Asoɔdenfo“Munnue mmofra asoɔdenfo,”sɛɛ na Awurade se,“Wɔn a wɔde nhyehyɛe a emfi me yɛ adwuma,na wɔyɛ apam a emfi me honhom mu,na wɔboaboa bɔne ano;
/// Yesaia 30:2
wɔn a wɔkɔ Misraima wommisa me ansa;wɔhwehwɛ mmoa a ɛyɛ bammɔ fi Farao nkyɛn,wɔkɔ Misraim kɔhwehwɛ hintabea.
/// Yesaia 30:3
Nanso Farao bammɔ bɛyɛ animguase ama mo,Misraim nwini de ahohora bɛbrɛ mo.
/// Yesaia 30:4
Ɛwɔ mu, wɔwɔ adwumayɛfo wɔ Soanna wɔn ananmusifo adu Hanes de,
/// Yesaia 30:5
nanso wɔn nyinaa anim begu asennipa bi a wɔn so nni wɔn mfaso nti,wɔmmfa mmoa anaa mfaso mma,na mmom animguase ne ahohora.”
/// Yesaia 30:6
Adehunu a ɛfa Negeb mmoa ho:wɔfa ahokyere ne ahohiahia asase so,nea gyata ne gyatabere,nhurutoa ne awɔ a wɔn ho yɛ hare wɔ,ananmusifo no de wɔn ahonyade soa mfurumne wɔn ademude wɔ yoma afu so,de kɔ saa ɔman a ne so nni mfaso no so,
/// Yesaia 30:7
de kɔ Misraim a ne mmoa so nni mfaso,enti mefrɛ noRahab nea Onsi Hwee.
/// Yesaia 30:8
Kɔ afei, kyerɛw wɔ kyerɛwpon so ma wɔn,kurukyerɛw wɔ nhoma mmobɔwee sosɛnea nna a ɛreba no mu noɛbɛyɛ adansede a ɛbɛtena hɔ afebɔɔ.
/// Yesaia 30:9
Saa nnipa yi yɛ atuatewfo, mma asisifo,mma a wɔmpɛ sɛ wotie Awurade nkyerɛkyerɛ.
/// Yesaia 30:10
Wɔka kyerɛ adehufo se,“Munhu mo ani so ade bio!”ne adiyifo no nso se,“Monnka anisoadehu a ɛyɛ nokware no nkyerɛ yɛn bio!Monka abodwosɛm nkyerɛ yɛn,monhyɛ nnaadaa nkɔm.
/// Yesaia 30:11
Mumfi saa ɔtempɔn yi so,momman mfi saa ɔkwan yi hona munnyae Israel Kronkronnia mode no haw yɛn yi!”
/// Yesaia 30:12
Enti sɛɛ na Israel Ɔkronkronni no se:“Esiane sɛ moapo saa nkra yi,na mode mo ho ato nhyɛsone nnaadaa so nti,
/// Yesaia 30:13
saa bɔne yi bɛyɛ sɛɔfasu tenten bi a ahyɛ kam na akyeana ebu mpofirim, ama mo.
/// Yesaia 30:14
Ebebubu nketenkete ayɛ sɛ kuku.Ɛbɛyam pasaa a,sin koraa renka awobetumi de asɔ gya afi muka muanaa wɔde bɛsaw nsu afi ahina mu.”
/// Yesaia 30:15
Sɛɛ na Otumfo Awurade, Israel Kronkronni no se:“Ahonu mu ne ntoboase mu na mo nkwagye wɔkommyɛ ne ahotoso mu na mo ahoɔden wɔ,nanso, morennya mu biara.
/// Yesaia 30:16
Mokaa se, ‘Dabi da, yɛbɛtena apɔnkɔ so aguan.’Enti mubeguan!Mokaa se, ‘yɛbɛtena apɔnkɔ a wɔn ho yɛ hare so akɔ.’Enti wɔn a wɔtaa mo no ho bɛyɛ hare!
/// Yesaia 30:17
Apem beguanɔbaako ho hu nti;baanum ho hu ntimo nyinaa beguan akɔ,akosi sɛ wobegyaw mote sɛ frankaa dua a esi bepɔw atifi,te sɛ frankaa a esi koko so.”
/// Yesaia 30:18
Nanso Awurade pɛ sɛ ɔdom mo;ɔma ne ho so, kyerɛ wo nʼahummɔbɔ.Efisɛ Awurade yɛ ɔtreneeni Nyankopɔn.Nhyira ne wɔn a wɔtwɛn no nyinaa!
/// Yesaia 30:19
Sionfo a mote Yerusalem, morentwa adwo bio. Sɛ musu frɛ no hwehwɛ mmoa a, ɔbɛdom mo! Sɛ ɔte a obegye mo so prɛko pɛ. 
/// Yesaia 30:20
Ɛwɔ mu, Awurade brɛ mo amanehunu sɛ aduan ne ɔhaw sɛ nsu de, nanso, afei mubehu Awurade, mo kyerɛkyerɛfo no bio, na ɔbɛkyerɛ mo kwan. 
/// Yesaia 30:21
Sɛ mofa nifa anaa benkum a, mobɛte nne bi wɔ mo akyi a ɛka se, “Ɔkwan no ni na momfa so.” 
/// Yesaia 30:22
Afei mobɛtow mo ahoni a mode dwetɛ adura ho ne nsɛsode a mode sikakɔkɔɔ afa ho no nyinaa agu. Mobɛtow wɔn agu sɛ ntamagow fi, na moaka kyerɛ wɔn se, “Mumfi yɛn so nkɔ.”
/// Yesaia 30:23
Afei ɔde osu behyira mo bere a mudua nnuaba, na aduan a asase no bɛbɔ no bɛyɛ papa na ɛbɛdɔɔso nso. Da no, mo anantwi bedidi wɔ adidibea a ɛtrɛw so. 
/// Yesaia 30:24
Mo anantwi ne mo mfurum a wofuntum asase no bɛwe atoko pa ne ne ntɛtɛ a, wɔde sofi ne nkorata trɛtrɛw mu. 
/// Yesaia 30:25
Saa okum kɛse da no a abantenten ahwehwe ase no, nsuwa bɛteɛ wɔ bepɔw tenten ne koko a ɛkorɔn biara so. 
/// Yesaia 30:26
Ɔsram bɛhyerɛn sɛ owia, na owia hyerɛn no bɛyɛ mpɛn ason, te sɛ nnafua ason mu hyerɛn, bere a Awurade kyekyere ne nkurɔfo akuru na ɔsa apirakuru a ɔde ama wɔn no.
/// Yesaia 30:27
Hwɛ! Awurade fi akyirikyiri reba,ɔde abufuwhyew ne wusiw kumɔnn;abufuw ahyɛ nʼanom ma,na ne tɛkrɛma yɛ ogya a ɛhyew nneɛma.
/// Yesaia 30:28
Ne home te sɛ asuworoe a ɛsen ntɛmntɛm,na ɛforo kɔdeda kɔn mu.Ɔwosow aman no wɔ ɔsɛe sɔnee so;ɔde nnareka a ɛma wɔfom kwanhyehyɛ nnipa no nnyepi.
/// Yesaia 30:29
Na mobɛto dwomte sɛ anadwo a mudi afahyɛ kronkron no;mo koma bedi ahurusite sɛ bere a nnipa de atɛntɛbɛn forokɔ Awurade bepɔw no so,kɔ Israel Ɔbotan no nkyɛn.
/// Yesaia 30:30
Awurade bɛma nnipa ate nʼabrɛmpɔnnnena ɔbɛma wɔahu ne basa a ɛresianna ɛde abufuwhyew ne ogya a ɛhyew nneɛmaosu dennen, aprannaa ne mparuwbo reba.
/// Yesaia 30:31
Awurade nne bɛhwe Asiria;ɔde nʼabaa bɛbɔ wɔn ahwe fam.
/// Yesaia 30:32
Ɔbɛbɔ wɔn wɔ ɔko mu,na bere biara a ɔbɛbɔ wɔn noɛbɛyɛ sɛ akasae ne sankuten so nnwonto koro.
/// Yesaia 30:33
Wɔasiesie Topet dedaada;wɔayɛ no krado ama ɔhene no.Wɔahyehyɛ ogya ne nnyentia bebreewɔ ne gya amoa no a emu dɔ na ɛtrɛw no mu;Awurade homete sɛ sufre asuten nobɛsɔ mu gya.

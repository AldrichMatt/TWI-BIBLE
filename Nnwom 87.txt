/// Nnwom 87:1
Dwom Koramma dwom.Wato ne fapem wɔ bepɔw kronkron no so;
/// Nnwom 87:2
Awurade dɔ Sion apon nosen Yakob atenae nyinaa.
/// Nnwom 87:3
Wɔka wo ho anuonyamsɛmOnyankopɔn kuropɔn:
/// Nnwom 87:4
“Mede Rahab ne Babiloniabɛka wɔn a wɔyɛ osetie ma me no ho,Filistia, Tiro ne Kus nsona maka se, ‘Wɔwoo eyinom wɔ Sion.’ ”
/// Nnwom 87:5
Ampa ara wɔbɛka afa Sion ho se,“Wɔwoo oyi ne ɔno wɔ no mu,na Ɔsorosoroni no ankasa de no betim hɔ.”
/// Nnwom 87:6
Awurade bɛkyerɛw wɔ nnipa dinkyerɛw nhoma no mu se,“Wɔwoo oyi wɔ Sion.”
/// Nnwom 87:7
Na wɔbɛto dwom se,“Me ntin nyinaa wɔ wo mu.”

/// Yesaia 16:1
Momfa nguantenmma sɛ sonkahirinkɔma asase no sodifo,efi Sela fa nweatam no so,kɔka Ɔbabea Sion bepɔw no so.
/// Yesaia 16:2
Te sɛ nnomaa a wɔaka wɔn afi berebuw muna wotu nenam wim nosaa na Moabfo mmea tewɔ Arnon asutwabea.
/// Yesaia 16:3
Moab ka se, “Munsi mo adwene pi,monna mo gyinaesi adi.Monyɛ mo sunsuma sɛ anadwo,awia ketee.Momfa aguanfo no nhintawmunnyi atubrafo no mma.
/// Yesaia 16:4
Momma Moabfo aguanfo no ne mo ntena;monyɛ wɔn bammɔ wɔ ɔsɛefo no ho.”Ɔhyɛsoni no bɛba nʼawiena adesɛe to betwa;opoobɔfo no bɛyera wɔ asase no so.
/// Yesaia 16:5
Ɔdɔ mu na Onyankopɔn besi ahengua bi;ɔbarima bi de nokwaredi bɛtena so,obi a ofi Dawid fi,nea atemmu mu no ɔpɛ atɛntreneena ɔntwentwɛn adetreneeyɛ ho.
/// Yesaia 16:6
Yɛate Moab ahantan a ɔyɛ,nʼani a atra ne ntɔn mmoroso,nʼahomaso ne nʼasoɔdennanso hwee nni nʼahomaso no mu.
/// Yesaia 16:7
Enti Moabfo twa adwo,wɔbɔ mu su ma Moab.Wodi awerɛhow na wɔbɔ abubuw,ma Kir Hereset bobe aba ɔfam.
/// Yesaia 16:8
Hesbon mfuw no hyew,Sibma bobe mfuw nso saa ara.Aman sodifo no atiatia bobe papa no so,nea kan na wɔde kɔ Yaserna ɛtrɛtrɛw kɔ nweatam so no.Wɔn mman trɛtrɛw kɔka mpoano pɛɛ.
/// Yesaia 16:9
Enti misu sɛnea Yaser su,de ma Sibma bobe mfuw.Hesbon ne Eleale,mede misu fɔw mo!Mo aduaba a abere ho ahurusine otwabere de no aka.
/// Yesaia 16:10
Ahosɛpɛw ne anigye nni mfikyifuw no mu;obiara nto dwom, na ɔnteɛteɛ mu wɔ bobeturo mu;obiara nkyi nsa wɔ nsakyiamoa so,efisɛ mede nteɛteɛmu aba nʼawiei.
/// Yesaia 16:11
Misi apini wɔ me koma mu wɔ Moab hote sɛ sankuten so kwadwom;na me yam hyehye me ma Kir Hereset.
/// Yesaia 16:12
Sɛ Moab kɔ ne sorɔnsorɔmmea aɔhaw ne ho kwa;sɛ ɔkɔ nʼabosonnan mu kɔbɔ mpae a,ɛrenkosi hwee.
/// Yesaia 16:13
Eyi ne asɛm a Awurade aka dedaw afa Moab ho. 
/// Yesaia 16:14
Nanso mprempren Awurade ka se, “Mfe abiɛsa mu, sɛnea akoa a nhyehyɛe kyekyere no no bebu nna no, wobebu Moab anuonyam ne ne nkurɔfo dodow no nyinaa animtiaa, na ne nkae no bɛyɛ kakraa bi a wonni ahoɔden.”

/// Yesaia 15:1
Nkɔmhyɛ A Etia MoabAdehu a ɛfa Moab ho:Wɔasɛe Ar a ɛwɔ MoabDa koro anadwo bi,wɔasɛe Kir a ɛwɔ Moabda koro anadwo bi!
/// Yesaia 15:2
Dibon foro kɔ nʼasɔredan mu,ɔkɔ ne sorɔnsorɔmmea kosu;Moab twa adwo wɔ Nebo ne Medeba ho.Wɔayi eti biara so nwina abogyesɛ biara nso wɔatwitwa.
/// Yesaia 15:3
Wofurafura atweaatam wɔ mmɔnten so;wɔ adan atifi ne ɔman ahyiaewɔn nyinaa retwa adwo,wɔdeda fam na wosu.
/// Yesaia 15:4
Hesbon ne Eleale teɛ mu su,wɔte wɔn nne kodu Yahas nohɔ.Enti Moab mmarima akofo teɛ mu su,na wɔbotobotow.
/// Yesaia 15:5
Midi Moab ho awerɛhow wɔ me koma mu;nʼaguanfo guan kodu Soar,ne Eglat Selisiya.Wɔforo kɔ Luhit,wɔrekɔ nyinaa na wɔresu;Horonaim kwan so nsowodi wɔn sɛe ho awerɛhow.
/// Yesaia 15:6
Nimrim nsuwansuwa ayoyowna sare no ahyewafifide nyinaa ayerana ɛnkaa wura biara a ɛyɛ amono.
/// Yesaia 15:7
Enti ahonyade a wɔapɛ agu hɔ nowɔsoa de kotwa mpampuro bon.
/// Yesaia 15:8
Wɔn nkekawmu gyigye wɔ Moab ahye so;wɔn agyaadwotwa kodu Eglaimna wɔn abubuwbɔ kodu Beer Elim.
/// Yesaia 15:9
Dimon nsu ayɛ mogya nko arananso mɛkɔ so ne no adigyata bɛba Moab aguanfo sone wɔn a wɔbɛka asase no so no so.

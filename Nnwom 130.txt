/// Nnwom 130:1
Dwom Ɔsoroforo dwom.Awurade, mifi bun mu su frɛ wo;
/// Nnwom 130:2
Awurade, tie me nne.Wɛn wʼasoma me nkotosrɛ.
/// Nnwom 130:3
Awurade, sɛ wubu yɛn bɔne ho nkontaa a,anka hena na obetumi agyina ano?
/// Nnwom 130:4
Nanso wowɔ bɔnefakyɛ;ɛno nti wosuro wo.
/// Nnwom 130:5
Metwɛn Awurade, me mu ade nyinaa twɛn,na nʼasɛm mu na mede mʼanidaso ahyɛ.
/// Nnwom 130:6
Metwɛn Awuradesen sɛnea awɛmfo twɛn adekyee,sen sɛnea awɛmfo twɛn adekyee.
/// Nnwom 130:7
Israel, ma wʼani nna Awurade so,efisɛ Awurade wɔ adɔe a enni huammɔna ogye ankasa wɔ ne mu.
/// Nnwom 130:8
Ɔno ankasa begye Israelafi wɔn bɔne nyinaa mu.

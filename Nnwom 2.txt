/// Nnwom 2:1
Dwom Adɛn nti na amanaman no hyehyɛ bɔne hona nnipa no bɔ pɔw hunu yi?
/// Nnwom 2:2
Asase so ahemfo sɔre gyinana sodifo ka wɔn ho bɔ mutia Awurade ne Nea Wɔasra no no.
/// Nnwom 2:3
Wɔka se,“Momma yemmubu wɔn nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔnna yɛntow wɔn mpokyerɛ ngu.”
/// Nnwom 2:4
Nea ɔte agua so wɔ ɔsoro no serew;Awurade di wɔn ho fɛw.
/// Nnwom 2:5
Afei ɔka wɔn anim wɔ nʼabufuw mu,hunahuna wɔn wɔ nʼabufuwhyew mu, ka se,
/// Nnwom 2:6
“Masi me Henewɔ Sion, me bepɔw kronkron no so.”
/// Nnwom 2:7
Mɛpae mu aka Awurade ahyɛde:Ɔka kyerɛɛ me se, “Woyɛ me Ba;nnɛ mayɛ wʼAgya.
/// Nnwom 2:8
Bisa me,na mede amanaman nyinaa bɛyɛ wʼagyapade,asase ano nyinaa bɛyɛ wo de.
/// Nnwom 2:9
Wode dade ahempema bedi wɔn so;na woabobɔ wɔn sɛ ɔnwemfo ahina.”
/// Nnwom 2:10
Enti mo ahemfo, munsua nyansa;monkae kɔkɔbɔ yi, mo asase sodifo.
/// Nnwom 2:11
Momfa osuro nsom Awuradena monsɛpɛw mo ho ahopopo mu.
/// Nnwom 2:12
Mumfew Ɔba no ano, na ne bo amfuwna mo akwan de mo bɛkɔ ɔsɛe mu,efisɛ nʼabufuwhyew tumi sɔre mpofirim.Nhyira ne wɔn a woguan toa no nyinaa.

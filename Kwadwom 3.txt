/// Kwadwom 3:1
Mene ɔbarima a mahu amanewɔ nʼabufuw abaa ano.
/// Kwadwom 3:2
Wapam me afi ne ho ama manantewsum mu na ɛnyɛ hann mu;
/// Kwadwom 3:3
Ampa ara wama nsa so atia mempɛn bebree, da mu nyinaa.
/// Kwadwom 3:4
Wama me were ne me nam anyinNa wabubu me nnompe.
/// Kwadwom 3:5
Waka me ahyɛ mu, na watua me anoɔde nweenwen ne ahokyere atwa me ho ahyia.
/// Kwadwom 3:6
Wama matena sum musɛ wɔn a wɔawuwu dedaw no.
/// Kwadwom 3:7
Wato ɔfasu atwa me ho ahyia enti mintumi nguan;wagu me nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn ama matɔ beraw.
/// Kwadwom 3:8
Mpo sɛ mefrɛ anaasɛ mebɔ mpae srɛ mmoa aosiw me mpaebɔ ano.
/// Kwadwom 3:9
Ɔde abotan asiw me kwan;wama mʼakwan ayɛ kɔntɔnkye.
/// Kwadwom 3:10
Te sɛ sisi a ɔda hɔ retwɛn,te sɛ gyata a watɛw,
/// Kwadwom 3:11
ɔtwee me fii ɔkwan no mu bebaree mena ogyaw me a minni mmoa biara.
/// Kwadwom 3:12
Okuntun ne ta muna ɔde ne bɛmma kyerɛɛ me so.
/// Kwadwom 3:13
Ɔde bɛmma a efi ne kotoku muhwirew me koma mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:14
Meyɛɛ akyiwade maa me nkurɔfo nyinaa;wɔto akutia nnwomde di me ho fɛw da mu nyinaa.
/// Kwadwom 3:15
Ɔde nhaban nweenwen ahyɛ me ma.Wama me bɔnwoma anom.
/// Kwadwom 3:16
Ɔde mmosea abubu me se;na watiatia me so wɔ mfutuma mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:17
Wɔama asomdwoe abɔ me;na me werɛ afi yiyedi.
/// Kwadwom 3:18
Enti mise, “Mʼanuonyam asa,nea mede mʼani too so Awurade mu no nyinaa.”
/// Kwadwom 3:19
Mekae mʼamanehunu wɔ mʼakyinkyinakyinkyin mu,nweenwen ne bɔnwoma mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:20
Mekae yiye,na me kra abotow wɔ me mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:21
Nanso midwen eyi ho;saa nti mewɔ anidaso.
/// Kwadwom 3:22
Awurade dɔ kɛse nti yɛnsɛee ɛ.Nʼayamhyehye nni huammɔ.
/// Kwadwom 3:23
Ɛyɛ foforo anɔpa biara;wo nokwaredi yɛ kɛse.
/// Kwadwom 3:24
Meka kyerɛ me ho se,“Awurade yɛ me kyɛfa, enti mɛtwɛn no.”
/// Kwadwom 3:25
Awurade ye ma wɔn a wɔn anidaso wɔ ne mu,onipa a ɔhwehwɛ no no;
/// Kwadwom 3:26
Eye sɛ wɔyɛ kommde twɛn Awurade nkwagye.
/// Kwadwom 3:27
Eye ma ɔbarima sɛ ɔsoa konnua nowɔ ne mmerantebere mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:28
Ma ɔntena ase komm,efisɛ Awurade de ato ne so.
/// Kwadwom 3:29
Ma ɔmfa nʼanim nsie wɔ mfutuma mu,ebia anidaso wɔ hɔ.
/// Kwadwom 3:30
Ma ɔmfa nʼafono mma nea ɔpɛɛ sɛ ɔbɔ no nona ɔnhyɛ no aniwu.
/// Kwadwom 3:31
Na Awurade ntow onipa biara nkyene koraa.
/// Kwadwom 3:32
Ɛwɔ mu, ɔde awerɛhow ba de, nanso obenya ayamhyehye.Ne dɔ kɛse no to rentwa da.
/// Kwadwom 3:33
Ofi amemenemfe mu de amanehunuanaa awerɛhow brɛ nnipa mma.
/// Kwadwom 3:34
Sɛ wɔde wɔn nan dwerɛwnneduafo a wɔwɔ asase no so a,
/// Kwadwom 3:35
sɛ wotiatia obi ahofadi sowɔ Ɔsorosoroni no anim a,
/// Kwadwom 3:36
anaa sɛ wobu obi ntɛnkyew a,Awurade renhu saa nneɛma yi ana?
/// Kwadwom 3:37
Hena na obetumi aka na wama aba muwɔ bere a ɛnyɛ Awurade na ɔhyɛe?
/// Kwadwom 3:38
Ɛnyɛ Ɔsorosoroni no anomna mmusu ne nnepa fi ana?
/// Kwadwom 3:39
Adɛn nti na ɛsɛ sɛ ɔteasefo nwiinwiibere a wɔatwe nʼaso wɔ ne bɔne ho?
/// Kwadwom 3:40
Momma yɛnhwehwɛ yɛn akwan mu na yɛnsɔ nhwɛ,na yɛnsan nkɔ Awurade nkyɛn.
/// Kwadwom 3:41
Momma yɛmma yɛn koma ne yɛn nsa so,nkyerɛ Onyankopɔn wɔ ɔsoro, na yɛnka se:
/// Kwadwom 3:42
“Yɛayɛ bɔne, na yɛatew atuana wamfa ankyɛ.
/// Kwadwom 3:43
“Wode abufuw akata wo ho ataa yɛn;na woakunkum a woannya ahummɔbɔ.
/// Kwadwom 3:44
Wode wo ho asie omununkum munti mpaebɔ biara nnu wo nkyɛn.
/// Kwadwom 3:45
Woayɛ yɛn atantanne ne nwurawɔ amanaman no mu.
/// Kwadwom 3:46
“Yɛn atamfo nyinaa abaa wɔn anom tɛtrɛɛde tia yɛn.
/// Kwadwom 3:47
Yɛabrɛ ne ahunahuna ne akukuruhwease,mmubui ne ɔsɛe.”
/// Kwadwom 3:48
Me nusu sen sɛ asutenefisɛ wɔasɛe me nkurɔfo.
/// Kwadwom 3:49
Me nusu bɛsen ara,na ɛrennyae,
/// Kwadwom 3:50
kosi sɛ Awurade bɛhwɛafi ɔsoro, na wahu.
/// Kwadwom 3:51
Nea mihu no ma me kra werɛ how,me kuropɔn no mu mmea nyinaa nti.
/// Kwadwom 3:52
Mʼatamfo a menyɛɛ wɔn hweepampam me sɛ anomaa.
/// Kwadwom 3:53
Wɔpɛɛ sɛ wotwa me nkwa sona wosiw me abo wɔ amoa mu;
/// Kwadwom 3:54
nsu bu faa me ti so,na ɛyɛɛ me sɛnea wɔrewie me.
/// Kwadwom 3:55
Mebɔɔ wo din, Awuradefii amoa no ase tɔnn.
/// Kwadwom 3:56
Wotee me sufrɛ: “Nsiw wʼasowɔ me gye sufrɛ ho.”
/// Kwadwom 3:57
Wutwiw bɛn me bere a mefrɛɛ wo no,na wokae se, “Nsuro.”
/// Kwadwom 3:58
Wo Awurade, wudii mʼasɛm maa me;na wugyee me nkwa.
/// Kwadwom 3:59
Woahu bɔne a wɔayɛ me, Awurade.Di mʼasɛm ma me!
/// Kwadwom 3:60
Woahu wɔn aweretɔ no mu den,wɔn pɔw a wɔbɔ tia me no nyinaa.
/// Kwadwom 3:61
Awurade, woate wɔn ntwirii,wɔn pɔw a wɔbɔ tia me no nyinaa,
/// Kwadwom 3:62
nea mʼatamfo ka no asomsɛm na wɔka no brɛoode tia me da mu nyinaa.
/// Kwadwom 3:63
Hwɛ wɔn! Sɛ wogyinagyina hɔ anaasɛ wɔtete hɔ,wɔto akutia nnwom de di me ho fɛw.
/// Kwadwom 3:64
Fa nea ɛfata tua wɔn so ka, Awurade,nea wɔn nsa ayɛ nti.
/// Kwadwom 3:65
Pirim wɔn koma,na ma wo nnome mmra wɔn so.
/// Kwadwom 3:66
Fa abufuw taa wɔn, na sɛe wɔnfi Awurade ɔsorosoro ase.

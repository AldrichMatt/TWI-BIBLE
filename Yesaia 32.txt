/// Yesaia 32:1
Trenee AhenniHwɛ, ɔhene bi bedi hene wɔ trenee muna ahemfo de atɛntrenee adi nnipa so.
/// Yesaia 32:2
Onipa biara bɛyɛ mframa ano hintabeane ahum mu guankɔbea,ɔbɛyɛ sɛ nsuwansuwa a ɛwɔ nweatam sone ɔbotan kɛse sunsuma a ɛwɔ osukɔm asase so.
/// Yesaia 32:3
Wɔn a wohu no ani renkata bio,na wɔn a wɔte asɛm no aso betie.
/// Yesaia 32:4
Nea ɔyɛ adwene mu hare behu na wate ase,wɔn a wɔpo dodow bɛkasa na mu ada hɔ.
/// Yesaia 32:5
Wɔremfrɛ ɔkwasea sɛ onuonyamfo biona wɔremfa nidi mma apapahwekwa.
/// Yesaia 32:6
Ɔkwasea ka nkwaseasɛmna osusuw bɔne ho:ɔyɛ anyame apɛdeɔka Awurade ho nsɛm a enye;ɔmma nea ɔkɔm de no no aduanɔde nsu kame nea osukɔm de no.
/// Yesaia 32:7
Apapahwekwa akwan yɛ amumɔyɛ,ɔyɛ bɔne ho nhyehyɛede atoro sɛe ohiani,wɔ bere a ohiani asɛm yɛ nokware mpo.
/// Yesaia 32:8
Nanso onuonyamfo yɛ nhyehyɛe a ɛho wɔ nyamna nneyɛe pa no nti ɔda nsow.
/// Yesaia 32:9
Yerusalem MmabaaMo mmea a mugye mo ho di,monsɔre na muntie me;mo mmabea a mususuw sɛ mowɔ bammɔmonyɛ aso mma nea mewɔ ka!
/// Yesaia 32:10
Afe akyi nna kakra bi nomo a mugye di sɛ mowɔ bammɔ ho bɛpopo;bobe twabere no bedi mo huammɔ,nnuaba no twabere nso remma.
/// Yesaia 32:11
Mo ho mpopo, mo mmea a mugye mo ho dimommɔ hu, mo mmabea a mugye di sɛ mowɔ bammɔ!Mompa mo ho ntamana momfa atweaatam mmɔ mo asen.
/// Yesaia 32:12
Mommobɔ mo koko so sɛ mowɔ mfuw afɛfɛne bobe a ɛso no nti,
/// Yesaia 32:13
ne me nkurɔfo asase,asase a nsɔe ne nnɛnkyɛnse agye afa.Yiw, montwa adwo mma afi a wogye wɔn ani wɔ mune saa ahosɛpɛw kuropɔn yi.
/// Yesaia 32:14
Wobegyaw aban no hɔ,na gyegyeegye kuropɔn no ada mpan;abanwa ne ɔwɛn aban bɛdan asasehunu afebɔɔ,nea mfurum pɛ ne nguankuw didibea,
/// Yesaia 32:15
kosi sɛ wobehwie honhom afi ɔsoro agu yɛn so,na nweatam bɛdan asaseberena asasebere ayɛ sɛ kwae.
/// Yesaia 32:16
Trenee bɛtena nweatam sona adetreneeyɛ atena asasebere so.
/// Yesaia 32:17
Adetreneeyɛ bɛsow asomdwoe aba;nea adetreneeyɛ de ba no bɛyɛ kommyɛ ne ahotoso a enni awiei.
/// Yesaia 32:18
Me nkurɔfo bɛtenatena mmeae a asomdwoe wɔafi a wɔabɔ ho banhomebea a ɔhaw biara nni hɔ.
/// Yesaia 32:19
Mpo sɛ mparuwbo sɛe kwae nona kuropɔn no sɛe koraa a,
/// Yesaia 32:20
wobehyira wo,wubedua wʼaba wɔ nsuwa biara ho,na wama wʼanantwi ne wo mfurum anantew baabiara.

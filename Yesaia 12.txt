/// Yesaia 12:1
Ayeyi NnwomSaa da no, wobɛka se:“Mɛkamfo wo, Awurade!Ɛwɔ mu wo bo fuw me de,nanso afei wʼabufuw ano adwo.Na woakyekye me werɛ.
/// Yesaia 12:2
Nokware, Onyankopɔn ne mʼagyenkwa;mede me ho bɛto no so, na merensuro.Awurade, Awurade ne mʼahoɔden ne me dwom;na wabɛyɛ me nkwagye.”
/// Yesaia 12:3
Wode anigye bɛsaw nsuafi nkwagye mmura mu.
/// Yesaia 12:4
Saa da no wobɛka se,“Da Awurade ase, bɔ ne dinma amanaman nhu nea wayɛ,na pae mu ka se wɔama ne din so.
/// Yesaia 12:5
To dwom ma Awurade, efisɛ, wayɛ nea ɛwɔ anuonyam bebree.Da eyi adi kyerɛ wiase nyinaa.
/// Yesaia 12:6
Momma mo nne so nto dwom anigye somo a mowɔ Sion,efisɛ Israel Kronkronni a ɔte mo mu no yɛ ɔkɛse.”

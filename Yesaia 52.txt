/// Yesaia 52:1
Nyan, nyan, Sion,hyɛ ahoɔden adurade no.Hyɛ wo ntade fɛɛfɛ noYerusalem kuropɔn kronkron no.Momonotofo ne wɔn a wɔn ho agu firenwura wʼapon mu bio.
/// Yesaia 52:2
Poroporow wo ho mfutuma.Sɔre, tena ahengua so, Yerusalem.Yiyi nkɔnsɔnkɔnsɔn no fi wo kɔn mu,Ɔbabea Sion a wɔafa wo nnommum.
/// Yesaia 52:3
Na sɛɛ na Awurade se:“Wɔantɔn wo annye hwee,na ɛnyɛ sika na wɔde begye wo.”
/// Yesaia 52:4
Na sɛɛ na Otumfo Awurade se:“Bere bi me nkurɔfo kɔɔ Misraim kɔtenaa hɔ;akyiri no Asiria bɛhyɛɛ wɔn so.
/// Yesaia 52:5
“Na mprempren dɛn na mewɔ wɔ ha?” Sɛɛ na Awurade se.“Wɔafa me nkurɔfo kɔ kwa,na wɔn sodifo di fɛw,”Awurade na ose.“Da mu no nyinaawɔkɔ so gu me din ho fi.
/// Yesaia 52:6
Enti me nkurɔfo behu me din;enti da no wobehu sɛɛyɛ me na mehyɛɛ ho nkɔm.Yiw, ɛyɛ me.”
/// Yesaia 52:7
Hwɛ sɛnea wɔn a wɔde asɛmpa no rebaanammɔn si yɛ fɛ wɔ mmepɔw no so,wɔn a wɔpae mu ka asomdwoesɛm,wɔn a wɔbɔ amanneɛdɛ,wɔn a wɔpae mu ka nkwagye ho asɛmna wɔka kyerɛ Sion se,“Mo Nyankopɔn di hene.”
/// Yesaia 52:8
Muntie! Mo awɛmfo ma wɔn nne so;wɔbɔ mu de anigye teɛ mu.Sɛ Awurade san ba Sion a,wɔde wɔn ani behu.
/// Yesaia 52:9
Mommɔ mu ntue anigye nnwonto,mo Yerusalem nnwiriwii,efisɛ, Awurade akyekye ne nkurɔfo werɛwagye Yerusalem.
/// Yesaia 52:10
Awurade beyi ne basa kronkron no howɔ amanaman nyinaa animna asase ano nyinaa behu yɛn Nyankopɔn nkwagye.
/// Yesaia 52:11
Monkɔ. Mumfi hɔ nkɔ!Mommfa mo ho nnka biribiara a ho ntew.Mumfi ho na mo ho ntew,mo a mukurakura Awurade nkuruwa.
/// Yesaia 52:12
Morentutu mmirika mfi haanaa morenguan;efisɛ Awurade bedi mo anim,Israel Nyankopɔn bɛbɔ mo kyidɔm.
/// Yesaia 52:13
Ɔsomfo No Amanehunu Ne NʼanuonyamHwɛ, me somfo bedi yiyewɔbɛma no so ayɛ no ɔkɛse.
/// Yesaia 52:14
Nea ɛmaa dodow a wohuu no ho dwiriw wɔn no,wɔsɛee nʼanim a ansɛ onipa biara dena nʼabɔsu sɛe a ɛnsɛ ɔdesani de,
/// Yesaia 52:15
saa ara na ɔbɛma aman bebree ho adwiriw wɔnna ahemfo bemuamua wɔn ano, ne nti.Wɔn a wɔnkaa ne ho asɛm nkyerɛɛ wɔn no behu,na wɔn a wɔntee no nso bɛte ase.
